This document describes the LPD-801 Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) Jammer.

The LPD-801 Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) Jammer is designed to suppress communication channels and data transmission from UAVs, as well as to suppress the navigation channel. The product can be used to disrupt the flight of the UAV in restricted areas, unauthorized transmission of UAV data to the operator, as well as to block radio devices based on Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, GPS, Glonass, Beidou and Galileo. Flight disruption is based on blocking the UAV control channel and the navigation channel. The product is equipped with a quick-detachable magazine-type battery pack, which allows you to quickly replace it.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation, Ukraine.
