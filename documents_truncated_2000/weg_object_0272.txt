This document describes the D-74 Russian 122mm Towed Gun.

The 122mm D-74 towed gun is a Soviet-built gun. Developed in the late 1950s it provided direct and indirect fire for the Soviet Army. Today it is in reserve units with the Russian Army.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Cambodia, China, Cuba, Egypt, Laos (People's Democratic Republic), North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea), Pakistan, Syria, Viet Nam, Zimbabwe.
