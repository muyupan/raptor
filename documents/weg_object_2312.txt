This document describes the Heckler and Koch P8 German 9mm Pistol.

The P8 is a modified version of the USP deployed by the German Bundeswehr. It differs by a specific safety and decocking as well as a preferred land and grooves rifling instead of the polygonal profile.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, Congo, France, Germany, Greece, Iceland, Lebanon, Luxembourg, Malaysia, Mexico, Norway, Pakistan, Portugal, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, Thailand, United Arab Emirates, United States of America, Uruguay.

Here is System information for the Heckler and Koch P8 German 9mm Pistol:
The Alternate Designation is P8.
The Manufacturer is Heckler and Koch.
The In Service is INA.
The Caliber is 9.
The Operating Principle is Recoil-operated with modified Browning locking system.
The Magazine Capacity is 15 rounds.
The Sights is 3-dot.
