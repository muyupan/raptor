This document describes the QJY-88 (Type 88) Chinese 5.8mm Light Machine Gun.

The QJY-88 is a machine gun, that is widely used by China's army. This weapon is also commonly referred to as Type 88. It was designed in the 1980s to replace the obsolete Type 67. Number "88" in the designation indicates the year when this weapon was developed.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
