This document describes the Supply Class Australian Replenishment Oiler.

The Supply Class Australian Replenishment Oiler is a class of two new vessels built by the Spanish company Navantia for the Royal Australian Navy (RAN). The vessels can be used to supply dry cargo, fuel, food, water, ammunition, equipment, and spare parts to deployed naval or combat forces during missions on the high seas.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Australia.
