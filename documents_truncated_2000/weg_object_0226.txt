This document describes the BrahMos Indian/Russian Short-Range Cruise Missile.

The BrahMos (designated PJ-10) is a short-range ramjet supersonic cruise missile that can be launched from a submarine, ship, aircraft, or land. It is the fastest supersonic cruise missile in the world. It is a joint venture between the Russian Federation and India, who together have formed BrahMos Aerospace.
It is based on the Russian P-800 Oniks cruise missile and other similar sea-skimming Russian cruise missile technology.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: India, Russian Federation.
