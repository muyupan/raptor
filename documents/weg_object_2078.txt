This document describes the Ultimax 100 Mk. 3 Singaporean 5.56mm Light Machine Gun.

The Ultimax 100 Mk. 3 Singaporean 5.56mm Light Machine Gun is a quick-change barrel. Currently, the Mark 3 variant is available in two different barrel lengths, a standard and short. The short barrel is designed for use with paratroopers and special forces. There is also an optional 10.5-inch (270 mm) VIP protection barrel.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bosnia and Herzegovina, Brunei Darussalam, Chile, Croatia, Fiji, Indonesia, Papua New Guinea, Peru, Philippines, Serbia, Singapore, Slovenia, Thailand, United States of America, Zimbabwe.

Here is Variants information for the Ultimax 100 Mk. 3 Singaporean 5.56mm Light Machine Gun:
The Mark 1 is Model with a quick-change barrel (pre-production)..
The Mark 2 is Equipped with a fixed barrel..
The Mark 3/3A is Quick-change barrel. Currently the Mark 3 variant is available in two different barrel lengths, a standard and short. The short barrel is designed for use with paratroopers and special forces. There is also an optional 10.5-inch (270 mm) VIP protection barrel. The barrel on all versions has a slotted flash suppressor and a carrying handle used to transport the weapon and assist in barrel removal..
The Mark 4 is Developed for the United States Marine Corps Infantry Automatic Rifle program, with the addition of a new fire selector module..
The Mark 5 is Updated variant of the Mark 4 with a folding stock, Picatinny rails and M16 STANAG 4179 magazine well that will accept 30-round box magazines and the 100-round Beta C-Mag drum..
The Mark 8 is New variant unveiled at the 2012 Singapore Airshow..
