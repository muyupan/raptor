This document describes the Eleron-3 Russian Reconnaissance Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Eleron-3 is a short-range tactical tail-less delta UAV with a maximum takeoff weight (MTOW) of 5.3kg carrying a 1kg payload. Manufactured by ENICS JSC it has a speed of 56 knots (104 km/h) and operates at a maximum altitude of 13,123 feet (4,000m). The Eleron-3 can perform autonomous, remote-controlled, and patrolling flights, and point observation missions. The UAV returns to the launch site autonomously and uses GPS and GLONASS signals for navigation.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
