This document describes the Charborz M3 Russian Arctic Buggy.

The Charborz M3 Arctic Buggy was developed by the Chechnya-based University of Spetsnaz, and is a variant of the Chaborz M3 all terrain combat buggy built at the Chechenauto factory in Chechnya. The first Chaborz vehicles were produced under the guidance of the Spetsnaz University in 2017. It is a belt-based vehicle with two front skies and double gun positions. It has a low center of balance but is reportedly very stable.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
