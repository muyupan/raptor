This document describes the Telemax Plus German Tracked Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).

Telemax PLUS
The biggest and strongest telemax:
Versatile and strong!
Our largest and strongest telemax, its exceptional ability to handle gradients and terrains makes it ideal for a particularly broad range of applications.

Features:
Exceptionally strong precision 6-axis manipulator with TCP control and automatic tool change;
Lifting capacity up to 80 kg;
Double payload area on the chassis;
4-track drive system with a top speed of up to 5 km/h;
More than 30 pre-programmed automatic motion sequences for manipulator and flippers;
Interchangeability of accessories within the telemax family, e.g. batteries, tools, cameras, and sensors

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Germany.
