This document describes the HMT 400 Supacat British High Mobility Transporter (HMT).

Already well-proven in the harsh terrain of the Afghanistan
battlefield, the 400 has a unique air suspension system that
allows the selection of a variable ride height to match the
terrain and also, enable internal loading within CH-47
Chinook.
Supplied with an optional mine blast and ballistic
protection kit, the 400 can be fitted with a variety of
mission specific hampers, weapons, communications, ISTAR
and force protection equipment to suit a wide range of
operational roles.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Australia, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
