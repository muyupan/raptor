This document describes the M3 German Self-Propelled Amphibious Bridging Vehicle .

The M3 German Self-Propelled Amphibious Bridging Vehicle is an amphibious bridging and ferrying system, used to carry tanks and other military vehicles across water obstacles. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Germany, Indonesia, Latvia, Singapore, South Korea (Republic of Korea), Sweden, Taiwan, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
