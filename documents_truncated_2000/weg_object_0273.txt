This document describes the RK-55 (SSC-X-4 'Slingshot') Russian Long-Range Cruise Missile.

The RK-55 (SSC-X-4 'Slingshot') Russian Long-Range Cruise Missile is the ground-launched component of the “55” series of cruise missiles which also included the Kh-55 air-launched missile. This family of cruise missiles was the Soviet Union’s response to the U.S. Tomahawk series.  The ground-launched variant was dismantled and destroyed due to the signing of the 1987 INF Treaty.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Belarus, China, India, Iran (Islamic Republic of), North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea), Pakistan, Ukraine.

Here is System information for the RK-55 (SSC-X-4 'Slingshot') Russian Long-Range Cruise Missile:
The Alternate Designation is RK-55, 3M10, Granat, SSC-X-4, Slingshot, 3K10, SS-CX-7/SS-CX-8.
The Launch Platform(s) is Submarines: Victor 3 (Project 671RTM), Akula 1/2 (Project 971), Sierra 1/2 (Project 945), Yankee Notch (Project 667AR/AT), and possibly Delta 1/2/3 class submarines; TEL based on the MAZ 543*.
The Basing is Submarine-launched, Ground launched from a TEL*.
The Effective Range is 2,400 km (submarine launch), 3,000 (ground launch)*.
The Circular Error Probability is 150.
The Class is Subsonic cruise missile.
The * Note is All ground launch versions of the RK-55 were to be destroyed within 3 years of the signing of the INF..

Here is Payload information for the RK-55 (SSC-X-4 'Slingshot') Russian Long-Range Cruise Missile:
The Warhead Type is Single.
The Warhead Options is Conventional and Nuclear.
The Nuclear Yield is 200.
The Conventional Warhead Weight is 410.
The Conventional Warheads Available is HE.
The Multiple Independently targetable Reentry Vehicle (MIRV) Capable is INA.

Here is Propulsion Characteristics information for the RK-55 (SSC-X-4 'Slingshot') Russian Long-Range Cruise Missile:
The Engine is R95-300 Turbofan.
The Propellant is Solid.
The Speed is 720.
The Flight Ceiling is INA.
The Attack Altitude is INA.

Here is Fire Control information for the RK-55 (SSC-X-4 'Slingshot') Russian Long-Range Cruise Missile:
The Guidance System is Sprut inertial guidance plus TERCOM.
