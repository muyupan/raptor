This document describes the Dongfeng CTL-181 Chinese 6x6 Light Tactical Vehicle.

The Dongfeng CTL-181 Chinese 6x6 Light Tactical Vehicle  is a 6×6 derivative of the CSK-181. It is fitted with a load tray at the rear of the hull and a single-row cab at the front. It is understood to be in service with the PLA and is used to carry UAVs and similar systems.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
