This document describes the JJ-7 Chinese Two Seat Combat Trainer Aircraft.

JJ-7/FT-7
This tandem two-seat trainer version was developed well after the single-seat fighters. It has an additional vertical fin to improve stability. An enlarged dorsal spine carries additional fuel.
First flight took place on July 5, 1985. The export model is known as the FT-7. Production of the JJ-7 was launched in late 1987.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
