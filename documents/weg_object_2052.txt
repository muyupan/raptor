This document describes the PP-87 (Type 87) Chinese 82mm Mortar.

The PP-87 (Type 87) Chinese 82mm Mortar is a Chinese infantry mortar developed by Norinco in 1987. It is a replacement for the older Type 67 82mm mortar used at the battalion level. An 81mm version called the W87 was also developed for export markets.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, China.

Here is System information for the PP-87 (Type 87) Chinese 82mm Mortar:
The Alternate Designation is PP-87; Type 87.
The Type is Mortar.
The Caliber is 82 mm.
The Manufacturer is NORINCO.
The Crew is 3.
The Length is 1.4 m.
The Weight of System is 27 kg.
The Elevation is 45 degrees to 85 degrees.
The Traverse is 4 degrees to the left and to the right.
The Rate of Fire is 20 r/min.
The Effective Firing Range is Between 120 m and 5.6 km.  6 km could be reached with an assisted round.

Here is Ammunition information for the PP-87 (Type 87) Chinese 82mm Mortar:
The Type is INA.
The Caliber is 82 mm.
