This document describes the T75 Taiwanese 5.56mm Light Machine Gun.

The T75 Taiwanese 5.56mm Light Machine Gun is produced by the 205th Arsenal, Ministry of Defense, Republic of China (Taiwan). The T75 is based on the proven Belgian-made FN Minimi LMG incorporating features from the T57 and T74 machine-guns with more modern features. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Taiwan.
