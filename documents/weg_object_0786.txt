This document describes the Hermes 450 Israeli Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Hermes 450 is an Israeli medium-sized multi-payload unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) designed for tactical long endurance missions. Its endurance is 17 hours, with a primary mission of reconnaissance, surveillance, and communications relay. Payload options include electro-optical/infrared sensors, communications, and electronic intelligence, synthetic-aperture radar/ground-moving target indication, electronic warfare, and hyperspectral sensors.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Azerbaijan, Botswana, Brazil, Colombia, Georgia, Israel, Mexico, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, United States of America, Zambia.
