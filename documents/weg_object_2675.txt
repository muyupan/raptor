This document describes the RN-94 Romanian 6x6 Amphibious Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The RN-94 Romanian 6x6 Amphibious Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) armor is an all-welded steel armor structure that provides maximum protection against 7.62 mm armor-piercing ammunition and shell splinters all around.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, Romania, Turkey.
