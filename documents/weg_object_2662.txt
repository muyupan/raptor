This document describes the VP11 Chinese 4x4 Mine-Resistant Ambush Protected (MRAP).

The VP11 Chinese 4x4 Mine-Resistant Ambush Protected (MRAP) is an MRAP-type vehicle built using a monocoque hull with a V-shaped bottom. It is designed to transport a body of troops into an area on patrol and for light combat missions. Alternatively, the vehicle could be used for local command, reconnaissance, and border security operations with differing mission systems as required by the customer.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Tajikistan, United Arab Emirates.
