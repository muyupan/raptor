This document describes the Lida-Class (Project 10750 Sapfir Class) Russian Coastal Minesweeper.

The Lida-Class (Project 10750 Sapfir Class) Russian Coastal Minesweeper is the successor to the YEVGENYA class MSIs. Lida Class (Project 10750 Sapfir Class) Russian Coastal Minesweeper can be used in mine warfare in waters up to 80.0 m in depth. Some 17-22 units were planned, but the program was terminated at nine units.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
