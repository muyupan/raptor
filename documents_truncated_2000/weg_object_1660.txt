This document describes the Hatsuyuki-Class Japanese Destroyer.

The Hatsuyuki is a class of destroyer deployed in the Japan Maritime Self-Defense Force (JMSDF). It was the first class of first generation, general-purpose destroyers in the JMSDF.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Japan.
