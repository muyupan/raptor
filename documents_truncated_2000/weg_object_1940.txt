This document describes the RPV-16 Ukrainian Thermobaric Rocket Launcher.

The RPV-16 is a Ukrainian thermobaric rocket launcher. It is a slightly improved version of the Soviet RPO-A Shmel. Mass production of the RPV-16 for the Ukrainian Armed Forces reportedly commenced in 2018. In 2020 hundreds of these single-use infantry flamethrowers were delivered to Ukrainian military.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.

Here is System information for the RPV-16 Ukrainian Thermobaric Rocket Launcher:
The Alternate Designation is RPV-16.
The Type is Thermobaric Rocket Launcher.
The Caliber is 93 mm.
The Manufacturer is Ukrainian state UkrOboronProm.
The Muzzle Velocity is 125 m/s.
The Maximum Range of Fire is 1,000 m.
The Range of Effective Fire is 300 m.
The Sighting Range is 600 m.
The Note is The RPV-16 man-portable thermobaric rocket launcher or flamethrower is meant for engagement of enemy personnel, placed both openly and in different structures, lightly-armored and soft-skinned vehicles, destruction of protected buildings, surface and semi-embedded structures of stone, bricks, or concrete..

Here is Dimensions information for the RPV-16 Ukrainian Thermobaric Rocket Launcher:
The Length is 920 mm.
The Diameter is INA.
The Weight is 11 kg.
