This document describes the FH70 British 155mm Towed Howitzer.

The FH70 (field howitzer for the 1970s) is a towed howitzer in use with several nations.  The FH-70 is a towed 155 mm howitzer developed by an international consortium, which included Vickers Ltd in the UK, OTO Melara in Italy, and Rheinmetall GmbH in West Germany. It was also manufactured in all three of these nations during its decade-long production run, and it was also produced in substantial quantity in Japan.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Estonia, Germany, Italy, Japan, Malaysia, Morocco, Netherlands, Oman, Saudi Arabia, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
