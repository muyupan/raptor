This document describes the SAPM Chinese Anti-Personnel Mine .

The SAPM Chinese Anti-Personnel Mine is a high-explosive (HE), scatterable, tripwire-actuated, anti-disturbance, anti-personnel (AP), fragmentation, landmine similar to the Russian POM-2, with self-destruct (SD) settings ranging 2 days 18 hours to 15 days 7 hours.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
