This document describes the Bell 214 American Medium-Lift Helicopter .

The Bell 214 American Medium-Lift Helicopter is a medium-lift helicopter derived from Bell Helicopter's ubiquitous UH-1 Huey series. The Bell 214ST shares the same model number, but is a larger, much-modified twin-engine derivative.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Australia, Iran (Islamic Republic of).
