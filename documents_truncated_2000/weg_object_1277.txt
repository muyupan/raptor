This document describes the FN-16 Chinese Short-Range Man-Portable Air-Defense System (MANPADS).

The FN-16 Chinese Short-Range Man-Portable Air-Defense System (MANPADS)  is a kind of new generation low altitude and short range air defense weapon upgraded from FN-6. The missile employs IR and UV dual color homing guidance seeker, the combination of laser proximity fuse and impact fuse. It has all-direction attack, anti-infrared decoy and “fire-and-forget” capabilities. It is used to intercept various types of attack helicopter, fixed-wing aircraft, cruise missile and UAV.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
