This document describes the T-14 Armata Russian Main Battle Tank (MBT).

T-14 Armata is a new-generation main battle tank (MBT) developed by Russian company Uralvagonzavod (UVZ). It was officially unveiled for the first time during the Moscow Victory Day Parade in May 2015.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.

Here is Variants information for the T-14 Armata Russian Main Battle Tank (MBT):
The T-16 Armata BREM is Armored recovery version with the T-14 chassis.  The standard turret of the T-14 is removed and replaced by a small, remotely operated, weapon station armed with a Kord 12.7mm caliber heavy machine gun.  A crane is mounted on the right side on the top of the hull which is protected at the front with slat armor..
