This document describes the Zastava NTV Serbian 4x4 Infantry Mobility Vehicle .

The Zastava NTV Serbian 4x4 Infantry Mobility Vehicle is a Serbian 4x4, multipurpose, all-terrain infantry mobility vehicle designed by the Military Technical Institute and Zastava Trucks, and manufactured by Zastava TERVO. The primary users are the Serbian Armed Forces.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Serbia.
