This document describes the T-20 (Arcturus T-20) American Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The T-20 is equipped with superior optics including premium imaging sensors such as Cloud Cap Technology (TASE 200 and 400 Series), gyro-stabilized EO/IR gimbal sensors with continuous zoom, onboard GPS/INS, and video processing. In addition, the T-20 features a common autopilot and ground control system architecture providing a highly customizable, modular platform to meet operational or customer requirements
