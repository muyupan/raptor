This document describes the Scania P340 Swedish 6x6 Cargo Truck.

The Scania P340 general utility truck is the replacement to the SAAB-Scania SBA-111. This medium general utility truck type was delivered to the Swedish Defense Force in 2005 with continued development of the chassis and cab in commercial models.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Northern Europe, Western Europe.
