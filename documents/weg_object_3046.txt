This document describes the GNOM Ukrainian 4x4 Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).

The GNOM Ukrainian 4x4 Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) can conduct covert surveillance using a circular survey camera on a telescopic mast.
The communication system and energy reserve allow for reconnaissance and surveillance at a distance of up to 5 km.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.

Here is System information for the GNOM Ukrainian 4x4 Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV):
The Alternative Designation is GNOM.
The Type is Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).
The Manufacturer is Timberland Military Solutions.
The Configuration is 4x4.
The Crew is Unmanned.
The Camera is Depth camera for operator control and autonomous movement;
360 ° panoramic camera on a telescopic mast;
IR illuminator (illumination for movement in the darkness)..
The Communication Channel is WiFi 5 GHz.
The Operating Temperature is – 25 ° to + 60 ° C.

Here is Dimensions information for the GNOM Ukrainian 4x4 Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV):
The Length is 600 mm.
The Width is 570 mm.
The Height is 380 mm.
The Weight (without Weapons and Ammunition) is 50 kg.
The Clearance is 90 mm.

The Engine Power is 5.44 hp.
