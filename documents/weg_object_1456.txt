This document describes the Chaho Class North Korean Patrol Boat.

The design of this class is based on the Russian P-6 torpedo boat design, but with a steel hull rather than the wooden hull of the P-6. Construction began in 1974 and ceased in the late 1970s. A minimum of 62 units were built during that period.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea).
