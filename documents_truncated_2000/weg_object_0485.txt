This document describes the Galatz Israeli 7.62mm Assault Rifle.

The Galatz Israeli 7.62mm Assault Rifle s designed to be issued to a designated marksman in a rifle squad, in order to extend the overall range, firepower, and accuracy of that formation, as opposed to traditional sniping missions.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Thailand.
