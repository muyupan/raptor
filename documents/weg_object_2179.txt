This document describes the VS-1.6 Italian Anti-Vehicle Mine .

The VS-1.6 Italian Anti-Vehicle Mine is a high-explosive (HE), scatterable, minimum-metal, anti-vehicle (AV), blast landmine which uses a fuze with an unusually high degree of shock and blast resistance with a direct copy also manufactured in Singapore.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Italy, Myanmar, Singapore.
