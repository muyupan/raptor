This document describes the VOG-25P Russian Anti-Personnel Grenade .

The VOG-25P Russian Anti-Personnel Grenade is  a high-explosive (HE), launcher-fired, airburst, anti-personnel (AP), bounding fragmentation, projected grenade employing the VMG-P point-detonating self-destruct (PDSD) fuze.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
