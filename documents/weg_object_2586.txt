This document describes the ZTL-11 (Type 11) Chinese 8x8 Armored Fighting Vehicle.

The ZTL-11 (Type 11) Chinese 8x8 Armored Fighting Vehicle is based on the type 08 8x8 wheeled armored vehicles family. It is the export variant of the ST1 vehicle, which is already in service with the Chinese Marine Corps.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Gabon, Nigeria, Thailand, Venezuela (Bolivarian Republic of).

Here is Variants information for the ZTL-11 (Type 11) Chinese 8x8 Armored Fighting Vehicle:
The ZBL-08 (Type 08) Infantry Fighting Vehicle is Original IFV variant with a 30 mm turret based on Shipunov 2A72. It features a crew cabin in the back for infantry transportation. HJ-73C missiles can be optionally mounted on the each side of the turret..
The ZSL-08 (Type 08) Armoured Personal Carrier is Based on IFV variant but the modified crew cabin has a higher ceiling. The Armored Personnel Carrier can be armed either with a manually controlled, armor-plate protected 12.7 machine gun or a remote-controlled weapon station..
The Type 08 Armored Reconnaissance Vehicle is Armed with a 30 mm turret, the vehicle has a chassis that is similar to the IFV variant. The Armored Reconnaissance Vehicle will advance before IFVs and assault guns for intelligence gathering. On-board high definition electro-optical cameras, thermal imaging system and laser designator can transmit battlefield and target information for other data-linked vehicles for target identification and designation. The Armored Reconnaissance Vehicle can provide better situational awareness for the infantry. The main radar and electro-optical sight is mounted on a retractable mast. There is also a version of the vehicle that does not have the 30 mm turret and uses the chassis of APC variant and has more cameras onboard but lacks self-defense weapons..
The PLL-05 Mortar Carrier is Based on IFV variant. It is armed with a 120mm dual purpose mortar..
The PLL-09 (Type 09) Modular Self-propelled Howitzer system is Based on IFV variant. Can be armed with either a 122mm or 155mm howitzer, to provide indirect fire support for infantry..
The Type 09 Anti-Air Reconnaissance Vehicle is Armed with a single barrel 35mm revolving auto cannon, the Air Defense variant has a unique chassis and is equipped with thermal-tracking sight, targeting and surveillance radars. The data collected by the radars can be data-linked back to command and armored reconnaissance vehicle..
The ZTL-11 (Type) 11 Assault Gun is Armed with a 105mm rifled gun, coaxial machine gun and 12.7mm commander machine gun, the Assault gun is based on the IFV chassis and can provide direct fire support for infantry battalions and engage enemy light vehicles and stationary targets such as bunkers..
The Type 08 Command Vehicle is With a modified chassis that has a much higher ceiling than the APC variant, the crew cabin provides a more spacious environment for commanders and staff. Equipped with satellite communication suite and battle management system, the command vehicle can move with the mechanized infantry for better battlefield management..
The Type 08 Communication Vehicle is Based on the Command Vehicle variant. It has additional communication equipment on board for the infantry battalion..
The Type 08 Armored Ambulance is Based on the Command Vehicle variant. Featuring a modified crew cabin with medical equipment, the Armored Ambulance variant has two Red Cross markers on each side of the vehicle body..
The Type 08 Hazardous Environment Reconnaissance Vehicle is Based on the Command Vehicle variant. Equipped with sensors and equipment for hazardous detection involving nuclear, biological and chemical environment..
The Type 08 Electronic Warfare Vehicle is Based on the Command Vehicle variant but the satellite communication suite on top of the vehicle roof is replaced by a rectangular shaped radar with several small radar panels for Electronic Support Measures..
The Type 08 Assault Breacher is Based on the IFV variant. It is equipped with mine plow and other mine detection device on board..
The Type 08 Mobile Bridge Builder is Based on the IFV variant. It is equipped with a vehicle-launched bridge on top of the hull..
The Type 08 Armored Recovery Vehicle is Based on the IFV variant, the turret is replaced by a crane for emergency vehicle service..
The Type 08 Armored Cargo Vehicle is Based on the APC variant, the Armored Cargo Vehicle has more bullet-proof windows on the vehicle body..
The Type 08 Engineering Vehicle is The vehicle has a unique chassis. It is equipped with tools for road paving and obstacle neutralization. A bulldozer blade is fitted on the front of the vehicle and an excavator bucket is mounted on the front-end of the roof. Waterline marks can also be found on the side of the vehicle body..
The VS27 is Armored rescue vehicle..
The VE36 is Reconnaissance vehicle..
The VE32A is Reconnaissance vehicle.
The CS/SA5 is Specialized air defense variant with a 6-barrel Gatling gun. The chassis of CS/SA5 is based on a modified Type 08 IFV. It comes with an air surveillance radar on the rear of the turret roof with a 2-axis adaptive-follow tracking radar situated on the starboard side of the surveillance radar while the thermal-tracking sight situated on the port side can provide visual information of target through fire control computers..
The CS/AA5 is Specialized IFV variants with a remote-controlled 40mm turret. The chassis of CS/AA5 is based on Type 08 IFV..
The VN-1 is Export variant based on Type 08 IFV. The VN-1C is armed with a 30 mm remote weapons station and a HJ-73 anti-tank missile.
The SWS-2 is Export SHORAD gun/missile system variant of the VN-1. It is armed with a 35 mm cannon and four surface-to-air missiles..
The ST-1 is Export variant based on Type 11 Assault gun, armed with a L7 105mm rifled gun..
The SH-11 is Export-oriented 155 mm self-propelled howitzer armed with a 39-caliber gun, which can be replaced with a 52-caliber gun without changing the chassis. It features a fully automatic loading system and the latest generation of optics..
The AFT-10 ATGM carrier is First shown at the 2018 Zhuhai Airshow..
