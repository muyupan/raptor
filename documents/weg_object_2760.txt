LTM 1090-4.2
Mobile crane
The mobile crane LTM 1090-4.2 combines mobility, performance and economy at the highest level. It is worldwide economically mobile, because it can be driven with 10 tons, 12 tons and 16 tons axle load. With its ECOdrive and ECOmode the LTM 1090-4.2 is operated extremely environmental friendly, low on consumption and with low noise. The Liebherr innovations VarioBallast and VarioBaseÂ® cater for more performance, flexibility and safety.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Australia, Germany, United States of America.

Here is System information for the Liebherr G-LTM 1090-4.2 German 35 Ton All-Terrain Crane:
The Alternative Designation is Liebherr G-LTM 1090-4.2.
The Type is 35 Tons All-Terrain Crane.
The Manufacturer is Liebherr.
The Crew is 2.
The Lifting Capacity is 36.6 tons.
The Boom is This crane has a powerful telescopic boom which extends to 35.7 m. It can lift loads with a maximum weight of 36.6 t. It can be used for medium and heavy crane work.  All functions of crane can be controlled from the crane cab or using a remote control unit..
The Recovery Winch is This mobile crane is fitted with a single recovery winch with 49 m of cable. The winch is mounted at the rear and can be used for recovery of stuck, swamped or damaged vehicles, as well as for self-recovery. If required the winch can be moved to the front..

Here is Protection information for the Liebherr G-LTM 1090-4.2 German 35 Ton All-Terrain Crane:
The Hull Armor is This crane has an armored driver's cab as well as the crane cab. Liebherr teamed with Rheinmetall Defence in order to develop and produce these cabs. The forward control cab is made of steel armor. The crane cab has a steel frame and ceramic armor in order for reduced overall weight. These cabs provide protection against small arms fire, artillery shell splinters and blasts. There is also an NBC protection system. The forward control cab has a roof hatch and can mount a machine gun for self-defense..
The Applique Armor is INA.
The Mine Clearing is No.
The NBC Protection is INA.
The Smoke Equipment is INA.
