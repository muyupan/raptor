This document describes the PHL-03 (Type 03) Chinese 300mm Multiple Rocket Launcher (MRL).

The PHL-03 Chinese 300mm Multiple Rocket Launcher (MRL) is a replica of the Russian 9K58 Smerch.  The PHL-03 MRL system consists of rockets, launch tube groups, launch vehicles, command vehicles, and transport vehicles.  A reload vehicle carries 12 rockets.  A PHL-03 unit consists of a command vehicle, 4-6 launch vehicles, and 4-6 reload vehicles.
The PHL-03 launcher can execute fire missions autonomously as each vehicle is equipped with GPS.
The rockets are not guided however, the munitions are.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Cambodia, China, Ethiopia, Morocco, Tanzania.

Here is Variants information for the PHL-03 (Type 03) Chinese 300mm Multiple Rocket Launcher (MRL):
The AR2 is Export variant that is similar to its parent..
The AR1 is Version of the PHL-03 with rearranged rocket tubes.  It was developed especially for export.  This artillery system has a total of 8 launch tubes, arranged in 2 pods with 4 rockets each.  The pods are factory fitted and sealed.  Once all of the rockets are launched the pods are removed and replaced.  Such artillery rocket systems are faster to reload, easier to store, and easier to maintain..
The AR1A is Has a total of 10 launch tubes arranged in 2 pods of 5 rockets each..
The AR3 is Has 8 launch tubes for 370mm guided rockets.  These are arranged in 2 pods with 4 rockets each.  These guided rockets have a maximum range of 220km and have capabilities similar to that of a short range ballistic missile..
