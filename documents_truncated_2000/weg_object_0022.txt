This document describes the 2S35-1 Koalitsiya-SV-KSh Russian 152mm Self-Propelled Howitzer (SPH).

The 2S35-1 Koalitsiya-SV KSh is a new Russian 152mm Self-Propelled Howitzer. Developed by JSC Central Research Institute Burevestnik, the 2S35-1 Koalitsiya-SV KSh is mounted on an 8x8 KAMAZ-6560 truck chassis and has the same turret and fires the same ordinance as its predecessor the 2S35-1 Koalitsiya-SV. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
