This document describes the BRDM-2DI "Hazar" Ukrainian Amphibious Armored Reconnaissance Vehicle.

BRDM-2DI "Hazar" Armored Reconnaissance and Patrol Vehicle is a modernized version of the vehicle BRDM-2. It is a two-axle, all-wheel drive vehicle, which has high dynamic qualities, extended cruising range, high cross-country capacity, and is amphibious. The vehicle is intended for the conduct of reconnaissance by fight and fire support of mechanized infantry sub-units.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.
