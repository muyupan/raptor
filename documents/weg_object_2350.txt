This document describes the Type 65 Chinese Projected Grenade .

The Type 65 Chinese Projected Grenade is a high-explosive anti-tank (HEAT), recoilless, fin stabilized, projected grenade incorporating a setback-armed, impact-inertia-fired, base-detonating (BD) fuze fired from a smoothbore recoilless gun.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
