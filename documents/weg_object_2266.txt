This document describes the M18-1 Iranian Anti-Personnel Mine.

The M18-1 Iranian Anti-Personnel Mine is a high-explosive (HE), claymore shaped, plastic-bodied, directional, anti-personnel (AP), landmine that is designed to wound or kill by its fragmentation and is a direct copy of the America M18A1 Claymore landmine.


This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, Iran (Islamic Republic of).
