This document describes the MGL Mk. 1 South African 40mm Grenade Launcher .

The MGL Mk. 1 South African 40mm Grenade Launcher is a lightweight 40 mm six-shot revolver-type grenade launcher (variations also fire 37/38mm) developed and manufactured in South Africa by Milkor (Pty) Ltd.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Azerbaijan, Bangladesh, Brazil, Central African Republic, Colombia, Croatia, Denmark, Georgia, India, Pakistan, Philippines, Rwanda, South Africa, South Korea (Republic of Korea), Sweden, Taiwan, Thailand, Turkey, Ukraine, United States of America, Viet Nam.
