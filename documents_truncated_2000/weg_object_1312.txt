This document describes the K263A1 South Korean 20mm Amphibious Self-Propelled Anti-Aircraft Gun.

The K263A1 is part of the KIFV family of fighting vehicles. It is based on the hull of the K200A1. The K263A1 is fitted with a turret which was originally developed for use on the American M163 Vulcan self-propelled anti-aircraft gun.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Malaysia, South Korea (Republic of Korea).
