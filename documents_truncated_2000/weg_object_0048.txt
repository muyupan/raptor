This document describes the ZTZ-98 (Type 98) Chinese Main Battle Tank (MBT).

Type ZTZ-98 (Type 98) Chinese Main Battle Tank (MBT) is armed with a fully-stabilized 125 mm smoothbore gun, fitted with an autoloader. Both the gun and autoloader were copied from the Soviet T-72 MBT. It has a maximum rate of fire of 8 rounds per minute. The Type 98 is capable of launching anti-tank guided missiles in the same manner as ordinary projectiles. It is compatible with a Russian 9K119M Refleks (Western reporting name AT-11 Sniper) anti-tank guided missiles. China manufactures these missiles under license. These anti-tank missiles can be fired when the tank is stationary and can also target low-flying helicopters.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
