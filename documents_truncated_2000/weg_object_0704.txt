This document describes the Wildcat British 4x4 Light Reconnaissance Vehicle (LRV).

The Wildcat was a high-performance off-road military vehicle developed by Qt Services in the U.K. British manufacturer Supacat partnered with Qt to militarize the vehicle, including systems integration and volume production, and market it for defense roles. This variant preceded the current Light Reconnaissance Vehicle 400.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
