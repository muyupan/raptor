This document describes the Mugin-4 Pro Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Mugin-4 Pro Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is designed based on the Mugen-2 Pro 2930 mm. With the same fuselage, the wingspan has been enlarged from 2930 mm to 4000 mm. The longer wingspan means the cruise speed will be reduced, and the flight time will be increased.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
