This document describes the T-64BM Bulat Ukrainian Main Battle Tank (MBT).

The T-64BM “Bulat” started as the Obyekt 447AM-1, this tank presented itself as a modernized version of the T-64BM. After studies of modernization in the early 2000s, work started at the Kharkiv Malyshev Factory design bureau by the first batch of 10 T-64B tanks converted to the BM “Bulat” standard in 2010. These were followed by 19 in 2011 and others in 2012, for a total of 76. The BM Bulat is the most comprehensive upgrade of the T-64B, it gives the T-64B the technology required to perform on a modern battlefield. Significant enhancements include the installation of an APU, increased thermal protection for the power pack, and an upgraded air-conditioning system.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.
