This document describes the Island Class Ukrainian Patrol Boat.

On 27 September 2017, two Island Class patrols boats, the USCGC Cushing and the USCGC Drummond, were transferred to the Ukrainian Navy, and renamed the P190 Sloviansk and the P191 Starobilisk. These boats are equipped with advanced electronics and navigation equipment and are used in support Ukraine's maritime security, defense operations, and search and rescue missions.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Georgia, Pakistan, Ukraine, United States of America.
