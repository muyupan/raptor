This document describes the WPF-89-1 Chinese Thermobaric Rocket Launcher .

The WPF-89-1 Chinese Thermobaric Rocket Launcher is a single-shot, shoulder-fired, disposable 80 mm rocket launcher fitted with a thermobaric warhead. The WPF89-1 uses the same launcher as the PF89 and PF89A weapons.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
