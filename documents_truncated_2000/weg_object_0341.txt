This document describes the M400 Vietnamese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The M400 Vietnamese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is a general-purpose, industrial-grade four-rotor Unmanned Aerial Vehicle platform designed for industrial inspections.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Viet Nam.

Here is System information for the M400 Vietnamese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV):
The Alternate Designation is M400.
The Type is Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).
The Manufacturer is Institute of Technology Air Defense by the Vietnamese Ministry of Defense.
The Autonomy is Semi-Autonomous.
The Crew is Unmanned.
The Payload Capacity is 1.2 kg.
The Reconnaissance Equipment is ClearCam Z10HD;
ClearCam Thermal;
ClearCam Z18HD;
ClearCam Z30HD.
The Operator Modes is First-person-view (FPV) mode;
Dual-operator mode.
The Software is 1. Plex Pilot (Ground control system, now available on Google Play Store)
– Waypoint-based autonomous operation
– Mobile mission planning
– Automated flight logging
2. Garuda Plex Fleet Management
– Web-based mission planning
– Deployment planning
– Equipment management
– Drone team management
– Integration for CAAS permit application.
The Launching System is Vertical Take-Off and Landing (VTOL).
The Recovery System is Vertical Take-Off and Landing (VTOL).
