This document describes the ZALA 421-16Е HD Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The ZALA 421-16Е HD Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is time-tested, the most popular type of UAV in the ZALA serial line. The increased carrying capacity of the aircraft allows the installation of additional equipment on board for multilevel analysis of the underlying surface.

It has a longer communication range of up to 100 km and is immune to interference. Launched from a pneumatic catapult, it has an improved aerodynamic shape. The air cushion protects the target load during landing.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
