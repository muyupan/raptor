This document describes the SC-90A Taiwanese 4x4 Combat Assault Vehicle .

The SC-90A Taiwanese 4x4 Combat Assault Vehicle is a four-wheel drive tactical all-terrain vehicle produced by Xingqiao Enterprise for the Army of the Republic of China . Its positioning is similar to foreign paratrooper assault vehicles (PAV) or fast assault vehicles (FAV). It can be quickly deployed using transport aircraft and helicopters. Its equipped firepower can also meet the requirements of light force reconnaissance and basic anti-armor capabilities.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Taiwan.
