This document describes the Tu-143 Reys (VR-3) Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Tu-143 Reys (Voyage) is a jet-powered tactical reconnaissance UAV. It is used for photographic, television and other surveillance missions by both military services and civilian authorities. The Reys is similar in appearance to the Tu-141 Strizh.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea), Russian Federation, Syria, Ukraine.
