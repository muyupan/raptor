This document describes the VN-11 Chinese Amphibious Infantry Fighting Vehicle (IFV).

The VN-11 is the export variant of the Chinese ZBD-04 IFV. The VN-11 layout, structure and performance is mostly the same as the ZBD-04; however it is assessed there are minor differences between the two platforms as a result of PLA operational security requirements. Although it is suspected that China may have sold the VN-11 to other countries, there are no verified exports of this platform.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
