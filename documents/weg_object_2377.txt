This document describes the M1001 American Projected Grenade .

The M1001 American Projected Grenade is a grenade-launcher-fired, high-velocity canister cartridge (HVCC), anti-personnel (AP), steel flechette (x107) loaded, projected grenade. The 40mm M1001 High Velocity Cartridge Canister (HVCC), fired from the MK19 Grenade Launcher, allows the operator to radically change use of the MK19 from a stand-off area weapon to a short-range anti-personnel and perimeter defense weapon. The M1001 HVCC is designed for applications such as Perimeter Defense, Close Range Self Protection and Police and Security Actions.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: United States of America.
