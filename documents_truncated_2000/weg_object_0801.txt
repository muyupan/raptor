This document describes the Tu-300 Korshun-U Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Tu-300 Korshun-U Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is an unmanned single-engine airplane with an aerodynamic "duck" scheme. For the lifting force corresponds to a triangular wing with a slight elongation. Mechanized communications and an electronic computer complex, as well as auxiliary and reconnaissance equipment, are installed in the forward part of the fuselage. The main load, missile-bomb armament or radio electronic equipment is installed on the external points of the fuselage suspension and in the fuselage compartment. In addition to its own weight of four tons, an aerial unmanned vehicle can take up to one ton of specialized load on board.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
