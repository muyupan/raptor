This document describes the PL-7 Chinese Air-to-Air Infrared Homing Missile.

The PL-7 (Chinese: 霹雳-7; pinyin: Pī Lì-7; literally: 'Thunderbolt-7') is the PRC version of the French Magic R.550 air-to-air missile. It is a short-range, Infrared homing missile used by Chinese fighters. It was designed by Wu Shendao, and produced at the Factory 331 (Zhuzhou Aeroengine factory).

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Myanmar, North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea), Sudan.

Here is PL-7 Air-to-Air Missile information for the PL-7 Chinese Air-to-Air Infrared Homing Missile of the System:
The Name is PL-7.
The Type is Air-to-Air Missile.
The Manufacturer is Factory 331 (Zhuzhou Aeroengine factory).
The Length is 2.74 m.
The Diameter is 157 mm.
The Wingspan is 0.66 m.
The Weight is 89 kg.
The Warhead is 12.5 kg.
The Speed is Mach 2.5.
The Range is 0.5 to 14 km.
The Guidance is Infrared homing.
