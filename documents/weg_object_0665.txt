This document describes the Kh-50 Russian Guided Missile.

The Kh-50 Russian Guided Missile is a 50 kg missile designed to be launched from Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV).

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.

Here is Dimensions information for the Kh-50 Russian Guided Missile of the System:
The Length is 1.8 m.
The Diameter is 180 mm.
The Fins is 300 mm.
The Weight is 50 kg.
