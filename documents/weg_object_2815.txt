This document describes the MDK-3 Russian Tracked Trench-Digging Vehicle.

The MDK-3 trench-digging machine (maschina dorozhnoy kopatelnoy) was developed as the Russian army ºs intended replacement of the MDK-2, and MDK-2M, full details of which can be found elsewhere in this section.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: CFE Treaty, Russian Federation, Ukraine.
