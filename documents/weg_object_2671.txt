This document describes the Lazar II Serbian 8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The Lazar II Serbian 8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) is an 8×8 multi-role military vehicle produced by the Complex Battle System factory in Velika Plana owned by Yugoimport–SDPR.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Pakistan, Serbia.
