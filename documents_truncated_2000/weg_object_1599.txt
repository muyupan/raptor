This document describes the Muna-Class (Project 1823 Class) Ukrainian Hydrographic Survey Vessel.

Project 1823 (type "Uhlomyer", Muna class by NATO classification), is a special purpose ship of the Naval Forces of Ukraine. It was originally named GS-13 under the Hydrographic Service of the Soviet Navy, but after the dissolution of the Soviet Union this vessel managed to be released from the Russian military.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation, Ukraine.
