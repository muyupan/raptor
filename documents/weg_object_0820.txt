This document describes the Mugin-6 Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Mugin-6 Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is designed based on the Mugin 4450 VTOL with an extended wingspan of 6000mm, in hope of reaching a longer flight time of 10 hours.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.

The Payload Capacity is 20 kg.
