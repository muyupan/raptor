This document describes the Ashok Leyland Stallion Mk.4 Indian 4x4 General Utility Truck .

The Ashok Leyland Stallion Mk.4 Indian 4x4 General Utility Truck is a further development of the previous Mk.3, further refined for desert type environments. It has a number of improvements and upgraded drivetrain. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: India, Nigeria.
