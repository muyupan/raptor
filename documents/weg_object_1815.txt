This document describes the Ashok Leyland Super Stallion Indian 8x8 Heavy Utility Truck .

The Ashok Leyland Super Stallion Indian 8x8 Heavy Utility Truck  is the new flagship of Ashok Leyland's range of military trucks. This high mobility vehicle was developed as a private venture to meet a possible requirement of the Indian Army.
