This document describes the M4-WAC-47 Ukrainian 7.62mm Assault Rifle.

The M4-WAC-47 Ukrainian 7.62mm Assault Rifle is a Ukrainian-made assault rifle created by Ukroboronprom and American company Aeroscraft (a division of Worldwide Aeros Corporation). It was tested within different sections of the Ukrainian Ground Forces

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.

Here is System information for the M4-WAC-47 Ukrainian 7.62mm Assault Rifle:
The Alternate Designation is M4-WAC-47.
The Type is Assault Rifle.
The Caliber is 7.62 mm.
The Manufacturer is Ukroboronprom and American company Aeroscraft (a division of Worldwide Aeros Corporation).
The Action is Gas Operated.
The Muzzle Velocity is 762 m/s.
The Rate of Fire is 800 rds/min.
The Maximum Effective Range is 351 m.
The Magazine is 30 rounds.
The Sights is Over-Receiver Optics; Flip up iron sights.

Here is Dimensions information for the M4-WAC-47 Ukrainian 7.62mm Assault Rifle:
The Length is 920 mm.
The Barrel Length is 675 mm.
The Weight, Empty is 3.60 kg.

Here is Ammunition information for the M4-WAC-47 Ukrainian 7.62mm Assault Rifle:
The Type is Rifle.
The Caliber is 7.62 mm.
The Cartridge is 7.62mmx39mm.
The Basic Load is INA.
