This document describes the M2 American 60mm Mortar.

The M2 American 60mm Mortar is a 60 millimeter smoothbore, muzzle-loading, high-angle-of-fire weapon used by U.S. forces in World War II, the Korean War, and the Vietnam War for light infantry support.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Albania, Australia, Austria, Bangladesh, China, Denmark, Egypt, Ethiopia, France, Greece, Guatemala, Haiti, Indonesia, Iraq, Lebanon, Mexico, Morocco, North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea), Pakistan, Portugal, South Korea (Republic of Korea), Thailand, Turkey, Ukraine, United States of America, Viet Nam.

Here is Variants information for the M2 American 60mm Mortar:

The Maximum Firing Range is 1.8 km.
