This document describes the Pari Class Indonesian Patrol Boat.

Pari-class is a Type PC-40 patrol boat of the Indonesian Navy.

There are a total of seven boats of this class,KRI Pari (849), KRI Sembilang (850), KRI Sidat (851), KRI Cakalang (852), KRI Tatihu (853), KRI Layaran (854), KRI Madidihang (855), KRI Krait (827).
