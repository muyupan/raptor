This document describes the Gurza-M-Class (Project 58155 Class) Ukrainian Inshore Patrol Boat.

The Project 58155 Gurza-M class is a series of small armored gun boats in service with the Ukrainian Navy. The first two vessels were laid down at the Kuznya na Rybalskomu (previously Leninska Kuznya) shipbuilding plant in October 2012. Originally it was planned to build nine such vessels by 2017. In December 2013 the Ministry of Defense withdrew its contract. In mid-2014 construction of the Gurza-M class was revived and the first two vessels were launched in late 2015. In December 2016 they officially joined the Ukrainian Navy. The new military contract for twenty vessels is to be completed by 2020.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.
