This document describes the KN-24 (Hwasongpho-11Na) North Korean Short-Range Ballistic Missile .

The KN-24 (Hwasongpho-11Na) North Korean Short-Range Ballistic Missile is a North Korean short-range ballistic missile (SRBM) first tested in August 2019. The missile flies on a quasi-ballistic trajectory.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea).
