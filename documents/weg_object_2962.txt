This document describes the M9 American Tracked Armored Combat Earthmover (ACE).

The M9 American Tracked Armored Combat Earthmover (ACE) is  is a highly mobile armored tracked vehicle that provides combat engineer support to frontline forces. Fielded by the United States Army, its tasks include eliminating enemy obstacles, maintenance and repair of roads and supply routes, and construction of fighting positions.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Taiwan, United States of America.
