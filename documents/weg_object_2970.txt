This document describes the CM-12 Taiwanese Main Battle Tank (MBT).

The CM-12 Taiwanese Main Battle Tank (MBT) is a modified M48A3 made for the Republic of China Army situated on Taiwan. The CM-12 boasts a sleek and robust design, integrating advanced technologies to enhance its combat effectiveness. With a low-profile silhouette and composite armor, it offers improved survivability against enemy fire. The tank’s turret is equipped with a state-of-the-art fire control system, enabling accurate target engagement even on the move. Additionally, the CM-12 incorporates modular armor, allowing for quick repairs and upgrades.
