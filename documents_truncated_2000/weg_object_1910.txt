This document describes the Type 79 Chinese 7.62mm Submachine Gun.

The Type 79 Chinese 7.62mm Submachine Gun is a selective-fire, air-cooled, magazine box-fed, gas-operated weapon of a conventional design. The Type 79s are configured for either automatic or semi-automatic fire via the use of a fire-selector plate. The Bulk of Type 79s appears to have been transferred to law enforcement units.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.

Here is System information for the Type 79 Chinese 7.62mm Submachine Gun:
The Alternate Designation is Type 79.
The Type is Submachine Gun.
The Caliber is 7.62mm.
The Manufacturer is NORINCO.
The Action is Gas, short-stroke piston.
The Mode of Fire is Selective, semi‐and full-auto.
The Cyclic Rate of Fire is 650 rd/min.
The Effective Firing Range is 200 m.
The Feed is Detachable, box magazine.
The Magazine is 20 Round per magazine.
The Forward Sights is Cylindrical post, adjustable.
The Rear Sights is Square notch, two position (100 and 200 m).
