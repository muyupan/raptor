This document describes the Evo II Dual 640T Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Evo II Dual 640T Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) has everything you need to take to the skies and fly missions. The 2-axis gimbal houses a dual-camera setup with a thermal and visual camera with the ability to film in stunning 8K quality.

The Thermal and Visual Cameras is The thermal camera on the 640T gives you the ability to view heat signature videos at up to 16x digital zoom. With the addition of a picture-in-picture mode that places thermal with visual footage, it's an essential tool to locate victims during a search and rescue, track suspects during a law enforcement pursuit, or find critical failures in equipment at plants and factories.

The visual camera transforms the EVO II Dual 640T into a filming powerhouse, letting you take stunning aerial video at up to 8K resolution, along with high-res 48MP still images. The visual camera offers crisp optical zoom up to 4x, with additional digital zoom past that up to 16x..
The GPS is GPS+BeiDou+Galileo（Asia） GPS+GLONASS+Galileo（Other areas）.
The Gimble is Three-axis stabilization.
The Launching System is Vertical Take-Off and Landing (VTOL).
The Recovery System is Vertical Take-Off and Landing (VTOL).
The Operating Temperature is -10℃ ~ 40℃.
The Accessories is Three of the included accessories in this bundle are a speaker, search light, and strobe attachment. These accessories expand what you can do with the EVO II Dual 640T when out in the field. You can use the speaker to communicate instructions to individuals or a crowd from the drone. You can use the search light to assist during night flights, or you can use the strobe during night flights so others can identify where the drone is in the sky..
