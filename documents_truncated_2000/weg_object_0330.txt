This document describes the DF-17 (Dongfeng 17) Chinese Medium-Range Ballistic Missile.

The Dongfeng 17 (DF-17) Chinese Medium-Range Ballistic Missile is a Chinese medium-range missile system equipped with a hypersonic glide vehicle. U.S. officials first confirmed the existence of DF-17 prototypes (DF-ZF/Wu-14) in 2014.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Pakistan.
