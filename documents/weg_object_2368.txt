This document describes the VGM-93.2 Russian Projected Grenade .

The VGM-93.2 Russian Projected Grenade is a grenade-launcher-fired, projected grenade with a CS gas irritant warhead, launched from the Russian magazine-fed grenade launcher GM-94 designed to produce a non-lethal effect on rioters and civil offenders. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation, Ukraine.
