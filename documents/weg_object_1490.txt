This document describes the Taechong II Class North Korean Patrol Craft.

The design of this class strongly resembles the Chinese HAINAN class, but has a lower superstructure and less freeboard than the HAINAN.
The vessels were completed between 1975 and 1995.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea).
