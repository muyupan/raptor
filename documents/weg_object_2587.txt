This document describes the BOV M16 Milos Serbian 4x4 Multipurpose Armored Vehicle .

The BOV M16 Milos Serbian 4x4 Multipurpose Armored Vehicle is a Serbian 4x4 multipurpose armored vehicle (MPAV) used for military and law enforcement applications. It features a V-hull, integrating floating-floor plates, and blast-mitigation seating to protect occupants against land mines and improvised explosive devices (IEDs).

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Cyprus, Serbia.
