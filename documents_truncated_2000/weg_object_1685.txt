This document describes the Kvant SPN-4 (1RL248-4) Russian High Power X-Band Radar Jammer.

The Kvant SPN-4 (1RL248-4) Russian High Power X-Band Radar Jammer is a derivative of the Ku-band SPN-2 jammer, built to operate in the 4 centimeter band. The systems share a considerable amount of hardware, but differ in the RF components of the designs.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
