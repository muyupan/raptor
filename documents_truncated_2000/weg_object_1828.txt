This document describes the LTSD-4-1 Russian Laser Rangefinder/Designator.

The LTSD-4-1 Laser Rangefinder/Designator is designed for ground target reconnaissance, correction of artillery and air firing in daylight and at night including the use of high-accuracy ammunition fitted with laser homing heads when illuminating the stationary and moving targets, military equipment and offensive fortifications by a laser light from ground observation points. It may be also used in automated fire control systems.
