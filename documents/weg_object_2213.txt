This document describes the TM-83 Russian Anti-Tank Mine.

The TM-83 Russian Anti-Tank Mine is a Soviet off-route shaped charge anti-tank mine, first shown publicly in 1993, and developed in 1983. The mine consists of a large Misznay Schardin effect warhead and is activated using its infra-red and seismic sensors. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation, Ukraine.
