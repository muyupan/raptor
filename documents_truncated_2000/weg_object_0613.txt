This document describes the ATAGS Indian 155mm Advanced Towed Artillery Gun System .

The ATAGS Indian 155mm Advanced Towed Artillery Gun System is a towed 155 mm/52 caliber howitzer that is being developed for the Indian Army by Armament Research and Development Establishment (ARDE), Tata Advanced Systems and Kalyani Strategic Systems. Bharat Forge, Tata Power SED and Advanced Weapons and Equipment India are manufacturing partners.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Armenia, India.
