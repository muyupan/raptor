This document describes the Shahid Haj Qasem Iranian Medium-Range Ballistic Missile .

The Shahid Haj Qasem Iranian Medium-Range Ballistic Missile is an Iranian ballistic missile which was unveiled on 20 August 2020. It is named after the Iranian commander Qasem Soleimani, who was assassinated by the US in January 2020.  

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Iran (Islamic Republic of).
