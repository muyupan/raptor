This document describes the Zala Kub Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Zala Kub Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is a Russian high-precision unmanned aerial vehicle and loitering munition developed by the Russian company ZALA Aero Group (part of Kalashnikov Concern). The military-grade drone can deliver a variety of weapon payloads with high precision during military operations based on manually set target coordinates or image-based target guidance payload. It can be used as a suicide drone or kamikaze drone to effectively destroy small-sized targets on the ground and sea.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
