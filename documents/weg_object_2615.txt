This document describes the CS/VP3 Chinese 4x4 Mine-Resistant Ambush Protected (MRAP).

The CS/VP3 is an armored 4 × 4 vehicle developed and produced by Poly Technologies from China. It is believed to be an export vehicle built with the intention to provide customers with a brand new armored platform at a lower cost than Western producers are able to achieve. It is in service with several African countries and Saudi Arabia. It is believed to be based on the Caprivi Mk 1 armored vehicle designed by Mobile Land Systems of South Africa.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Kenya, Nigeria.
