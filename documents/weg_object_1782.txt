This document describes the Bogdan 2351 Ukrainian 4x4 Light Utility Vehicle.

The Bogdan 2351 Ukrainian 4x4 Light Utility Vehicle is a tactical utility vehicle, produced in Ukraine. It is based on a Chinese Great Wall Wingle 5 civilian pickup. Some modifications were made to the vehicle in order to adapt it for military use.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.
