This document describes the Zala Rex-2 Russian Anti-Drone Gun.

The Zala Rex-2 Russian Anti-Drone Gun suppresses the control channels between the operator and the drone, as well as the drone's satellite navigation.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.

Here is System information for the Zala Rex-2 Russian Anti-Drone Gun:
The Alternate Designation is Zala Rex-2.
The Type is Anti-Drone Gun.
The Manufacturer is Zala Aero.
The Operating Time is 2 hours.
The Suppression Radius of Communication Signals, Control and Data Transmission (30°) is 0.5 km.
The Signal Suppression Radius of Satellite Navigation Systems: NAVSTAR (GPS), GLONASS, GALILEO, BeiDou (360°) is 2.0 km.
The Operating Temperatures is -40°C -  +50°C.
The Note is The REX-2 anti-drone gun is equipped with three signal suppression modules for 2.4 GHz and 5.8 GHz frequencies as well as for satellite navigation signal suppression. According to the company, the frequencies can be changed depending on the customer’s demand..

Here is Dimensions information for the Zala Rex-2 Russian Anti-Drone Gun:
The Length is 530 mm.
The Width is 90 mm.
The Height is 280 mm.
The Weight is 3.8 kg.
