This document describes the Throwbot 2 American Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).

The Throwbot 2 American Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) is a throwable micro-robot platform that enables operators to obtain instantaneous video and audio reconnaissance within indoor or outdoor environments.  Designed to withstand repeated drops of up to 30 feet (9 m) onto concrete, the Throwbot 2 robot can be thrown into hazardous situations in order to allow operators to quickly make informed decisions when seconds count.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: United States of America.
