This document describes the BMM-3 Russian 8x8 Amphibious Armored Mobile Dressing Station.

The BMM-3 operates as the mobile dressing station with the team of doctors and a bandaging set.

The basic design of all modifications comprises a basic sanitary set. Up to four heavy casualties can be carried on stretchers inside the hull, and additional two outside on the stretchers placed under engine compartment roof.

The equipment and medicine set ensures the delivery of first aid at stationary position and on the move. The vehicle has a folding tent to be attached to the vehicle at any side door. Up to 12 casualties can be accommodated in the tent.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
