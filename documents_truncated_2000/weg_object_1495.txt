This document describes the Alexadrit Obukhov Class (Project 12700 Class) Russian Minesweeper.

The Alexandrit-class (Project 12700) mine countermeasures vessels are being built by Sredne-Nevskiy Shipyard, a subsidiary of United Shipbuilding Corporation, for the Russian Navy. The vessels are designed to detect and destroy mines at naval bases and harbors.

The keel for the lead vessel in class, Alexander Obukhov (507), was laid at Sredne-Nevskiy Shipyard in St Petersburg, Russia, in September 2011. The vessel was launched in June 2014 and commissioned into the Baltic fleet in December 2016.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
