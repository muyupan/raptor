This document describes the Leeuwin Class Australian Hydrographic Survey Ship.

The Leeuwin Class Australian Hydrographic Survey Ship is a two-ship class of hydrographic survey vessels operated by the Royal Australian Navy (RAN). Leeuwin and Melville were ordered from NQEA Australia in 1996, and were commissioned in 2000. The ships are capable of charting waters up to 6,000 meters deep, carry three Fantome-class survey boats, and can operate an AS 350B Squirrel helicopter.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Australia.
