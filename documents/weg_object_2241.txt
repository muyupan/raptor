This document describes the PMN-3 Russian Anti-Personnel Mine.

The PMN-3 Russian Anti-Personnel Mine a high-explosive (HE), circular plastic-bodied, delay armed, battery-powered, electronic, anti-disturbance, self destruct, pressure or tilt actuated, anti-personnel (AP), landmine which externally looks like the PMN-2 anti-personnel (AP) landmine and is intended to impede landmine clearance.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
