This document describes the RKG-3EM Russian Anti-Tank Grenade .

The RKG-3EM Russian Anti-Tank Grenade a high-explosive anti-tank (HEAT), hand-thrown, parachute-stabilized, impact-inertia-fired, hand grenade that has a larger warhead and boasts a penetration of 220mm.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
