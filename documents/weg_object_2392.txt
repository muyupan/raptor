This document describes the UAG-40 Ukrainian 40mm Automatic Grenade Launcher.

The UAG-40 Ukrainian 40mm Automatic Grenade Launcher is one of the many domestic arms developments to come out of Ukraine in recent years. It was developed and is currently manufactured by Kusnja na Rybalskomu, a shipyard and major arms manufacturer in Kiev, located on the banks of the Dnieper River. Kusnja na Rybalskomu, known as Leninska Kuznya since 1924, changed its name in 2017 in accordance with new Ukrainian regulations which do not permit references from Soviet times in company names.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.

Here is System information for the UAG-40 Ukrainian 40mm Automatic Grenade Launcher:
The Alternate Designation is UAG-40.
The Type is Automatic Grenade Launcher.
The Caliber is 40 mm.
The Manufacturer is Kusnja na Rybalskomu,.
The Action is Blow back.
The Rate of Fire is 370-400 rounds/minute.
The Muzzle Velocity is 240 m/s (varies with ammunition).
The Maximum Range of Fire is 2,200 m (varies with ammunition).
