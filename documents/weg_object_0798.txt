This document describes the WZ-7 Xiang Long (Soaring Dragon) Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The WZ-7 Xiang Long (Soaring Dragon) Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) features a unique joined-wing design. The primary mission is expected to be aerial reconnaissance, but it may also be fitted to provide targeting data for anti-ship ballistic missiles and cruise missiles.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
