This document describes the Bayraktar Mini Turkish Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Bayraktar Mini Turkish Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) has been developed as Turkey's first mini-robot system which is completely original with national electronics, software and structural components of the aircraft. The system, which was developed with the hard work and effort of our R & D team, passed all the tests successfully and was first put into service of the Turkish Armed Forces in 2007. Mini UAV System, which has many advanced features compared to its competitors developed in foreign countries, can be used even under difficult geographical and meteorological conditions.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Turkey.

Here is System information for the Bayraktar Mini Turkish Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV):
The Alternate Designation is Bayraktar Mini.
The Type is Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).
The Manufacturer is Bayraktar.
The Autonomy is Fully Autonomous with Sensor Fusion Assisted.
The Crew is Unmanned.
The Payload Capacity is INA.
The Camera is 2 Axis Day / Night Camera.
The Launching System is Hand shot (Automatic Takeoff).
The Recovery System is Automatic Return to Home and Automatic Overhead Landing / Automatic Parachute Landing; Automatic Landing in Case of Communication Loss.
