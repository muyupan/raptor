This document describes the KamAZ-6560 Russian 8x8 Heavy Utility Truck.

The KamAZ-6560 Russian 8x8 Heavy Utility Truck is one of the most powerful trucks, produced by KamAZ. It was first publicly revealed in 2005.  Initially this military truck was aimed at the export customers, however later it was also proposed for the Russian Army.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Kazakhstan, Russian Federation.
