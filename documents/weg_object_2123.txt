This document describes the C6A1 FLEX Canadian 7.62mm General-Purpose Machine Gun.

C6A1 FLEX is a Canadian 7.62x51mm NATO general-purpose machine gun. The C6A1 is an improved version of C6 that is a Canadian version of FN MAG. The C6A1 FLEX replaced the C6 in the Canadian military in 2019. Improvements include polymer buttstock, M1913 Picatinny rails, and adjustable gas tube regulators.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Canada, New Zealand.
