This document describes the ZSD-89 (Type 89) Chinese Amphibious Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The ZSD-89 (Type 89) Chinese Amphibious Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) is a tracked armored personnel carrier. It is a successor of the Type 63 armored personnel carrier. The Type 89 has many design details in common with the Type 63, however the it is slightly larger overall. It was manufactured by NORINCO and powered by a German Deutz diesel. The Type 89 is in service with the Chinese Army.

   The base model of the Type 89 is fully amphibious with propulsion once in the water being provided by the tracks. Troops enter the vehicle via a single door in the hull rear. Once inside there are several ball-and-socket firing ports for the occupants, one being in the entry door, and periscopes are provided to allow observation of the battlefield for the occupants.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Albania, Iraq, North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea), Sudan, Tanzania, Viet Nam.

The Type YW307 - Type 89 Infantry Fighting Vehicle is Fitted with a one-man turret with 25 mm automatic gun (400 rounds) and 7.62 mm coaxial machine gun (1,000 rounds). Crew: 3+7. Military designator: ZSD-89-II..
The Type 89 Armoured Command Vehicle is Fitted with a higher roofline and with a longer chassis with 6 double road wheels either side. Armament consists of a 12.7 mm HMG on the commander's station behind the driver..
The Type WZ752 - Type 89 Tracked Armoured Ambulance is Unarmed ambulance with a higher roofline and with a longer chassis with 6 double road wheels either side..
The Type 89 Refuelling Vehicle is Transport vehicle for fuel and lubricants, fitted with a higher troop compartment. Offered for export as the Amphibious Armoured Refuelling Vehicle..
The ZJX-93 - Type 89 Recovery Vehicle is Maintenance and recovery variant, fitted with a light crane on the left side of the hull roof, and a machine gun cupola in the roof center..
The ZHB-94 - Type 89 Supply Vehicle is Similar to the recovery variant and also equipped with a light crane, but the commander's cupola with HMG is located behind the driver's position..
The Type WZ731 - Type 89 Reconnaissance Vehicle is Variant for artillery forward observers, equipped with an unarmed turret with observation devices. Military designator: ZZC-01..
The Type 89 Recce Radar Carrier is Equipped with a battlefield surveillance radar on a telescopic arm. Military designator: ZZC-02..
The Type 89 Tank Destroyer (missile) is ATGM carrier vehicle with double launch system for wire-guided HJ-8 missiles. A total of 12 missiles is carried on board. Military designator: ZDF-1..
The Type 89 Mine-Laying Vehicle is Has a modified rear hull that mounts a scatterable mine system, consisting of 6 launchers. Each launcher has 36 tubes of 122 mm with each tube containing 5 SATM mines, 15 SAPM mines, 45 SAPEM mines or a mix of SAPM and SAPEM mines..
The Type 89 Obstacle-Removing Vehicle is Crowd-control variant for the PAP, equipped with an obstacle clearing blade..

The YW309 is infantry fighting vehicle, with a BMP-1-style 73mm low-pressure smoothbore gun and a HJ-73 anti-tank guided missile launcher. Prototype only..
