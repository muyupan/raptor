This document describes the Tata LPTA 5252 Indian 12x12 Special Wheeled Chassis .

The Tata LPTA 5252 Indian 12x12 Special Wheeled Chassis was as a private venture to meet a possible Indian Army requirement. It is intended to carry various missiles, such as Prahar, Brahmos and Nirbhay.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: India.
