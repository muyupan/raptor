This document describes the OTO Melara Mod 56 Italian 105mm Towed Howitzer.

The OTO Melara Mod 56 Italian 105mm Towed Howitzer is an Italian-made 105 mm pack howitzer built and developed by OTO-Melara. It fires the standard US-type M1 ammunition.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Argentina, Bangladesh, Botswana, Brazil, Chile, Ecuador, Ghana, Greece, India, Italy, Kenya, Malaysia, Nepal, Nigeria, Pakistan, Peru, Philippines, San Marino, Spain, Sudan, Thailand, Venezuela (Bolivarian Republic of), Zambia.

Here is System information for the OTO Melara Mod 56 Italian 105mm Towed Howitzer:
The Alternative Designation is OTO Melara Mod 56.
The Type is Towed Howitzer.
The Caliber is 105 mm.
The Manufacturer is OTO-Melara.
The Carriage is Split trail.
The Crew is 7.
The Breech is Vertical sliding-block.
The Recoil is Hydro-pneumatic.
The Elevation is -7° to +65°.
The Traverse is 56°.
The Muzzle Velocity is 416.0 m/s.
The Rate of Fire is 10 rounds/min.
The Maximum Firing Range is 10,000 m.
The Emplacement Time is 2-3.
The Displacement Time is 2-3.

Here is Dimensions information for the OTO Melara Mod 56 Italian 105mm Towed Howitzer:
The Length is 3.65 m.
The Barrel Length is 1.47 m.
The Width is 1.5 m.
The Height is 1.9 m.
The Weight, Combat is 1,200 kg.

Here is Ammunition information for the OTO Melara Mod 56 Italian 105mm Towed Howitzer:
The Type is Projectile.
The Caliber is 105 mm.
