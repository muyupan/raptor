This document describes the BAT-2 Russian Combat Engineer Vehicle.

The BAT-2 armored tracklayer is used for road and track clearing and grading. It can drive graded tracks across various terrain and snow. The vehicle is also used to create earth barriers, dig ditches. It can be also used during disaster relief operations. The BAT-2 is also described as a combat engineering vehicle due to its versatility.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Hungary, Russian Federation, Ukraine.
