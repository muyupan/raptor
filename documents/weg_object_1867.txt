This document describes the Khaibar-1 Syrian Unguided Artillery Rocket.

HEZBOLLAH Proliferation. 
The Khaibar-1 Syrian Unguided Artillery Rocket is best known for being used by Hezbollah against targets in northern Israel during the 2006 Lebanon War, and has also been used in the Syrian Civil War. It is a derivative of the Chinese WS-1 rocket.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Iran (Islamic Republic of), Non-State Actors, Syria.
