This document describes the RG-42 Russian Anti-Personnel Grenade .

The RG-42 Russian Anti-Personnel Grenade is a high-explosive (HE), fragmentation, anti-personnel (AP), defensive hand grenade originally introduced during World War II as an emergency measure, continuing in use with Russian forces in the post-war period, also made in Albania and designated the Model or Type 42.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Albania, Russian Federation.
