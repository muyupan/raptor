This document describes the Perdana Menteri Class Malaysian Diesel-Electric Submarine.

The PERDANA MENTERI class of submarines is part of Malaysia's effort to catch up with neighboring countries, such as Singapore and Indonesia, which have been acquiring submarines. The boats of expected to be used for anti-submarine warfare (ASW), special operations, intelligence collection and surveillance operations.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Malaysia.
