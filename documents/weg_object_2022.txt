This document describes the KN-23 (Hwasong-11Ga) North Korean Short-Range Ballistic Missile .

The KN-23 (Hwasong-11Ga) North Korean Ballistic Missile is a North Korean short-range ballistic missile (SRBM). The missile flies on a quasi-ballistic trajectory and has a maximum range of 690 km.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea), Russian Federation.
