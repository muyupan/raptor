This document describes the FHJ-84 Chinese Double-Barrel Incendiary Rocket Launcher.

The FHJ-84 Chinese Double-Barrel Incendiary Rocket Launcher features two tubes that can be loaded with 62mm incendiary and smoke rockets. The FHJ-84 is China's attempt to develop an alternative to World War II era flamethrowers.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
