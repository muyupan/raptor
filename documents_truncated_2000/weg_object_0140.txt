This document describes the SNAR-2 (Pork Trough) Russian Mobile Counter-Battery Radar.

Pork Trough** (SNAR-2) is a Russian counter-battery radar used to locate enemy artillery and provide position information. It employs a rotating horizontal parabolic cylinder antenna mounted on a fully tracked AT-LM artillery tractor.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Azerbaijan, Belarus, Bulgaria, Moldova, Russian Federation.
