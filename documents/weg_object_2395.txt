This document describes the Type 74 Chinese Flamethrower.

The Type 74 Chinese Flamethrower is man portable flamethrower of Chinese origin. The design is very similar to the Soviet LPO-50. The Type 74 was developed to increase effectiveness while reducing the weight at the same time.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Ukraine.

Here is System information for the Type 74 Chinese Flamethrower:
The Alternate Designation is Type 74.
The Type is Flamethrower.
The Caliber is 14.5 mm.
The Manufacturer is NORINCO.
The Recoil Force is 65 kg.
The Ignition is 2x incendiary cartridge near muzzle.
The Trigger Type is Electrical trigger.
The Power Source is Replaceable battery in flame gun.
The Reloading Time is 4 to 6 minutes.
The Firing Range is 45 meter direct fire range.
The Expulsion Time is 2 to 3 seconds per cylinder.

Here is Dimensions information for the Type 74 Chinese Flamethrower:
The Length is 0.85 m.
The Weight is 20 kg complete system with full tanks.

Here is Fuel information for the Type 74 Chinese Flamethrower:
The Fuel Type is Gasoline with thickening powder.
The Caliber is 14.5 mm.
The Cylinder Capacity is 4 liters per cylinder.
The Number of Cylinders is 3.
