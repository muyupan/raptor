This document describes the PU-12M7 Russian 8x8 Mobile Command and Control Vehicle.

The PU-12M7 is an improved variant of the PU-12M6. It has improved sensors and upgraded air defense command and control capabilities. The system is normally found in a missile regiment at divisional level, controlling up to five subordinate surface-to-air missile firing batteries.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: CFE Treaty, Russian Federation.
