This document describes the T112 Taiwanese 5.56mm Sniper Rifle.

The T112 Taiwanese 5.56mm Sniper Rifle is a Taiwanese AR-15–style rifle produced by the 205th Armory. It is intended to replace the T91 assault rifle. The new rifle will augment and replace the current 5.56x45mm T91 carbine, which entered service in 2003. Like the T91, and the T65 before it, the T112 is a gas-operated weapon with a rotating bolt. Development reportedly began in 2020 at Taiwan’s 205th Arsenal of the Armaments Bureau which specialises in small arms development.



This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Taiwan.
