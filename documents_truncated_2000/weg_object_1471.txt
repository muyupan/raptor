This document describes the Hayabusa-Class Japanese Guided Missile Patrol Boat.

The Hayabusa is a guided missile patrol boat class of the Japan Maritime Self-Defense Force (JMSDF). Six boats were built between 2002 and 2004. This patrol boat provides the JMSDF a fast response ship-to-ship missile capability and has a full suite of enhanced offensive and defensive systems.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Japan.
