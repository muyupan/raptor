This document describes the BREM-84 (Atlet) Ukrainian Armored Recovery Vehicle.

The BREM-84 (Atlet) Ukrainian Armored Recovery Vehicle is designed to repair and recover damaged infantry fighting vehicles (IFVs) and combat equipment on the battlefield. It is also used to tow damaged vehicles to the maintenance depots.

The first prototype was built in 1997 and the vehicle was accepted for service with the Ukrainian Army in November 2008. Serial production of the vehicle began in April 2018 to meet the requirements of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Pakistan, Thailand, Ukraine.
