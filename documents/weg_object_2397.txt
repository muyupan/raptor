This document describes the 9M119 Svir / 9M119M Refleks (AT-11 Sniper) Russian Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM).

The 9M119 Svir, 9M119M Refleks, and AT-11 Sniper by NATO are laser beam riding, guided anti-tank missiles developed in the former Soviet Union. The two missiles are similar but vary in range and launch platform. Both are designed to be fired from smoothbore 125 mm tanks and anti-tank guns (2A45, 2A46, and 2A46M). Their NATO reporting name is AT-11 Sniper. The name Svir comes from the River Svir, while Refleks means reflex. The 9M119 replaces or supplants, the 9K112 Kobra.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, China, Cyprus, Georgia, India, Morocco, Russian Federation, Viet Nam.
