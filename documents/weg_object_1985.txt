This document describes the Heckler & Koch HK33 German 5.56mm Assault Rifle.

The Heckler & Koch HK33 German 5.56mm Assault Rifle is a 5.56mm assault rifle developed in the 1960s by West German armament manufacturer Heckler & Koch GmbH (H&K), primarily for export.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Brazil, Chile, Ecuador, El Salvador, Germany, Ghana, Greece, Guatemala, Indonesia, Iran (Islamic Republic of), Ireland, Malaysia, Mexico, Myanmar, Netherlands, Papua New Guinea, Peru, Philippines, Portugal, Saudi Arabia, Senegal, South Africa, Spain, Sweden, Tanzania, Thailand, Turkey, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States of America.
