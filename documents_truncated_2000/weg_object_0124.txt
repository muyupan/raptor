This document describes the Dauwoo K3 South Korean 5.56mm Light Machine Gun.

The Dauwoo K3 South Korean 5.56mm Light Machine Gun resembles the FN Minimi and uses a standard 5.56×45mm NATO cartridge. Its greatest advantage is that it is lighter than the M60 and can interchange cartridges with both the K1A and K2.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Colombia, Ecuador, Fiji, Guatemala, Indonesia, Philippines, South Korea (Republic of Korea), Thailand.

Here is Variants information for the Dauwoo K3 South Korean 5.56mm Light Machine Gun:
The XK3 is Prototype..
The K3 is Standard version..
The K3Para is Shortened version of K3 with RAS and minor modifications. First revealed in 2015..
The K15 is Heavily redesigned and modernized version..

Here is System information for the Dauwoo K3 South Korean 5.56mm Light Machine Gun:

The Type is Light Machine Gun.
The Caliber is 5.56 mm.
The Manufacturer is Daewoo Precision Industries, current S&T Motiv..
The Action is Gas-operated, rotating bolt.
The Rate of Fire is 700 rounds/min belt fed
