This document describes the TB-001 Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The TB-001 Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is dubbed the Twin-Tailed Scorpion, is a strike and reconnaissance unmanned air vehicle (UAV) developed by Tengoen of China to meet the requirements of the People's Liberation Army (PLA) and export customers.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
