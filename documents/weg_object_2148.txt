This document describes the Type 72 (Metallic) Chinese Anti-Vehicle Mine.

The Type 72 (Metallic) Chinese Anti-Vehicle Mine is a high-explosive (HE), circular, sheet metal-bodied, anti-vehicle landmine which is designed to damage or destroy a vehicle by its blast effect a copy of the Russian TM-46 with a few modifications.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
