This document describes the DF-4 (Dongfeng 4) Chinese Intermediate-Range Ballistic Missile.

The Dongfeng 4 (DF-4/CSS-3) Chinese Intermediate-Range Ballistic Missile (IRBM) is a two-stage, transportable, liquid-fueled intermediate to intercontinental ballistic missile (IRBM/ICBM) that entered service in 1980. It was expected to be decommissioned by 2005, but one brigade remains operational near Lingbao, Henan province.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.

Here is Dimensions information for the DF-4 (Dongfeng 4) Chinese Intermediate-Range Ballistic Missile:
The Length is 28.05 m.
The Diameter is 2.25 m.
The Fin Span is INA.
The Launch Weight is 82,000 kg.
