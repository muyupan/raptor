This document describes the F1 Australian Anti-Personnel Grenade.

The F1 fragmentation hand grenade is manufactured by Thales Australia and is used by the Australian Defense Force.



This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Australia.

Here is Dimensions information for the F1 Australian Anti-Personnel Grenade:
The Length is 96.
The Diameter is 58.
The Weight is 375.
The Filling Weight is 62.
