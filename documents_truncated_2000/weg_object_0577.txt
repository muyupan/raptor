This document describes the Panhard AML-90 French 90mm Self-Propelled Gun (SPG).

The Panhard AML-90 French 90mm Self-Propelled Gun (SPG) is a direct fire support variant of the AML family. It is armed with a large-caliber gun designed to be used against armored vehicles and emplacements.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Argentina, Bahrain, Burkina Faso, Burundi, Cameroon, Chad, Djibouti, Ecuador, El Salvador, Gabon, Guinea, Kenya, Lebanon, Lesotho, Malaysia, Mauritania, Morocco, Niger, Nigeria, Rwanda, Saudi Arabia, Senegal, Somalia, Sudan, Togo, Tunisia, Venezuela (Bolivarian Republic of), Yemen.
