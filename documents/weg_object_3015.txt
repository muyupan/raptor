This document describes the RTK-10 Russian 4x4 Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).

The RTK-10 Russian 4x4 Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) is a small unmanned ground vehicle designed to be used in environments with CBRN contaminants. It is capable of conducting analysis of what contaminants are present as well as some reconnaissance of structures.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.

Here is System information for the RTK-10 Russian 4x4 Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV):
The Alternative Designation is RTK-10.
The Type is Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).
The Manufacturer is PTK, State Research Centre of Russia.
The Configuration is 4x4.
The Crew is Unmanned.
The Operator is INA.
The Operating Temperature is -20 to 30°C.
