This document describes the GJT 211 Type 82 Chinese Tracked Bulldozer.

The GJT 211 Type 82 Chinese Tracked Bulldozer was designed from the outset for combat engineer use. Development began in 1979 and it is based on the running gear and automotive of the Type 62 light tank and was preceded by the visually similar GJT 210. Following issues with early production examples, full production of the Type 82 commenced in 1983.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
