This document describes the Xian MA60 Chinese Regional Airliner.

The Xian MA60 Chinese Regional Airliner is a turboprop-powered airliner produced by China's Xi'an Aircraft Industrial Corporation under the Aviation Industry Corporation of China (AVIC).

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Angola, Bolivia (Plurinational State of), Burundi, Cambodia, Cameroon, China, Congo, Djibouti, Eritrea, Laos (People's Democratic Republic), Sri Lanka, Yemen, Zambia, Zimbabwe.
