This document describes the DJI MAVIC 3M Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) .

The DJI MAVIC 3M Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is a powerful new drone weighing 951g. Packed with a Multispectral Camera and RGB Camera and it has a 43min flight time. It has features like Omnidirectional Obstacle Avoidance and 14.54km video transmission. As of 2023, this is the leading agricultural drone to scan and analyze crop growth.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Russian Federation, Ukraine.
