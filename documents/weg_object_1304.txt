This document describes the Pasars-16 Serbian 6x6 Short-Range Air Defense System .

The Pasars-16 Serbian 6x6 Short-Range Air Defense System is a Serbian mobile hybrid short range air defense system intended for the protection of infantry, armored mechanized and artillery-missile units from low flying aircraft, cruise missiles, unmanned aerial vehicles and other projectiles.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Serbia.
