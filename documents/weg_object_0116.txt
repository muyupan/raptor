This document describes the B-300 Israeli 82mm Anti-Tank Rocket Launcher.

The B-300 Israeli 82mm Anti-Tank Rocket Launcher was developed in the 1970s, entered service with the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) in 1980, and remains in active service with every nation that has so far adopted it.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Azerbaijan, Chile, Estonia, India, Israel, Mexico, Singapore, Trinidad and Tobago, United States of America.
