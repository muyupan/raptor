This document describes the T-64BK Russian Main Battle Tank (MBT).

The T-64BK is a command variant of the T-64B. It is designed for local command of armored vehicles at the tactical level. It is equipped with additional communications equipment for this purpose. The T-64BK can be differentiated from a T-64B by the positioning of a longer antenna on the turret. The antenna may be secured to the turret with additional cables and when fully extended can be over 10 m long.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation, Ukraine, Uzbekistan.
