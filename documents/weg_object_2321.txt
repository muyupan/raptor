This document describes the BK-881 Russian Anti-Tank Grenade .

The BK-881 Russian Anti-Tank Grenade is  a high-explosive anti-tank (HEAT) fin-stabilized, projected grenade also made in Poland, Bulgaria and North Korea with the BK-881M being the same grenade except with a copper shaped-charge (SC) cone, intended to defeat armored targets with the training version being designated PBK-881.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
