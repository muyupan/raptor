This document describes the Type 69-II Chinese Main Battle Tank (MBT).

The Type 69-II Chinese Main Battle Tank (MBT) became the production standard MBT of the Type 69 family, it was developed primarily for the export market and remains in service with countries throughout the world despite its age. It is an upgrade of Type 59 and Types 69 detailed below, however, it is on a par with improved Type 59s.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, Iran (Islamic Republic of), Myanmar, Thailand, Zimbabwe.
