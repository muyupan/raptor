This document describes the YM-1B (YM-IB) Iranian Anti-Personnel Mine.

The YM-1B (YM-IB) Iranian Anti-Personnel Mine is a high-explosive (HE), scatterable, blast-resistant, anti-personnel (AP), blast landmine incorporating an over-pressure mechanism preventing initiation in the event of sudden shock or impact. It is externally like the YM-1 but amalgamates features from the Italian TS-50 and VS-50 landmines.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Iran (Islamic Republic of).
