This document describes the Type 87 Chinese Projected Grenade .

The Type 87 Chinese Projected Grenade is a series of projected grenades, spin-stabilized, with various warheads.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.

Here is Warheads  information for the Type 87 Chinese Projected Grenade  of the System:
The DFS87 is Anti-personnel grenade with HE-Frag warhead..
The DFJ87 is Armor-piercing round with HEAT warhead..
The DFR87 is Incendiary grenade..
The DFN87 is Anti-personnel incendiary grenade..
The DFD87 is Smoke marker grenade, using coloured smoke..
