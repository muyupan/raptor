This document describes the K-300P Bastion-P (SS-C-5 Stooge) Russian Coastal Defense Missile System .

The K-300P Bastion-P (SS-C-5 Stooge) Russian Coastal Defense Missile System primary role is to engage various surface ships. It can also engage carrier battle groups, convoys or landing craft. In some cases missiles fired from this system can target surface targets.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Belarus, Russian Federation.
