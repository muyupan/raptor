This document describes the Nora B-52 Serbian 155mm Self-Propelled Howitzer.

The Nora B-52 Serbian 155mm Self-Propelled Howitzer was developed to replace older artillery systems in service with the Serbian Army.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, Cyprus, Greece, Kenya, Myanmar, Serbia.
