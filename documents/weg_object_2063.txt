This document describes the UB M52 Serbian 120mm Mortar.

The UB M52 Serbian 120mm Mortar is a 120 mm (4.75 inch) mortar that was developed by Military Technical Institute Belgrade.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, El Salvador, Macedonia, Myanmar, Serbia, Slovenia, Ukraine.

Here is Variants information for the UB M52 Serbian 120mm Mortar:
The M52 is Basic Version.
The M52A1 is Improved Version.
The M52A2 is Improved Version.
The M52A3 is Improved Version.

Here is System information for the UB M52 Serbian 120mm Mortar:
The Alternate Designation is UB M52.
The Type is Mortar.
The Caliber is 120 mm.
The Manufacturer is PPT-Namenska AD.
The Crew is 5.
The Rate of Fire is 25 rpm.
The Maximum Firing Range is 7,460 m.
The Effective Firing Range is 6,100 m.
The Feed System is Manual.
