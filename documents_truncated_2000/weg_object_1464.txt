This document describes the Gumdoksuri Class South Korean Guided Missile Patrol Boat.

The GUMDOKSURI-class high-speed patrol boats are designed to replace South Korea's aging PAEK KU 52- and CHAMSURI-class patrol boats. It is being procured in two variants: the PKX-A, equipped with missile systems; and the PKX-B, armed only with guns (PKX stands for the Patrol Killer Experimental program).

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: South Korea (Republic of Korea).
