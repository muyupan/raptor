This document describes the BREM-2 Russian Armored Repair and Recovery Vehicle (ARRV).

The BREM-2 armored repair and recovery vehicle (ARRV) is a conversion based on the hull of the Russian-designed BMP-1 Infantry Fighting Vehicle (IFV).

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Algeria, Angola, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Bulgaria, Cambodia, CFE Treaty, Congo, Cuba, Czech Republic, Egypt, Eritrea, Ethiopia, Finland, Georgia, Greece, Hungary, India, Iran (Islamic Republic of), Iraq, Kazakhstan, North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea), Poland, Romania, Russian Federation, Slovakia, Ukraine.
