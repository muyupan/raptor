This document describes the T91 Taiwanese 5.56mm Assault Rifle.

The T91 Taiwanese 5.56mm Assault Rifle was adopted by the Taiwanese armed forces in 2003. By 2008 it replaced all service rifles, including the older T65 series, in all branches of the Taiwanese military. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bosnia and Herzegovina, Gambia, Haiti, India, Jordan, Kuwait, Taiwan, United Arab Emirates.
