This document describes the Jebat Class Malaysian Frigate.

The Jebat Class is a Lekiu-class guided missile frigate currently serving in the Royal Malaysian Navy and one of the major naval assets for Malaysia. Jebat serves in the 23rd Frigate Squadron of the Royal Malaysian Navy.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Malaysia.
