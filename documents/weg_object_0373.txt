This document describes the Ural-63708 Russian 8x8 Heavy Equipment Transport Truck.

The Ural-63708 is a new Russian tractor truck with an 8x8 configuration. It is a version of the Ural-63704-0010 military truck with an added axle at the rear and fitted with a more powerful engine. It was first publicly revealed in 2016. This tractor truck is a member of the Tornado-U family of military vehicles. In this case letter "U" in the designation stands for "Ural", as there is also a similar KamAZ-U family, developed by another Russian truck manufacturer KamAZ.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
