This document describes the MAZ-6317 Belarusian 6x6 Heavy Utility Truck.

The MAZ-6317 Belarusian 6x6 Heavy Utility Truck was developed in the late 80s to meet the requirements of the Soviet military. First prototype was completed in 1986. The new truck was intended to replace KrAZ-255B heavy-load carrier.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Belarus, Russian Federation.
