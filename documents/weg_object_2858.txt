This document describes the BTR-60-R-145BM Russian 8x8 Command Post and Communication Center Vehicle.

The BRT-60-R-145BM Russian Command Post and Communication Center Vehicle  is a variant of the BTR-60 that has removed the turret and equipped the vehicle with a R-245 radio and other communications equipment. Additional equipment includes: Two x R-111/171, one x R-123/173, one x R-130M and one x R-8xx ground-to-air radio sets.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Kazakhstan, Kurdistan, Russian Federation, Tajikistan.
