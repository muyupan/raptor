This document describes the Flakpanzer Gepard German Self-Propelled Anti-Aircraft Gun.

The Flakpanzer Gepard German Self-Propelled Anti-Aircraft Gun is an all-weather-capable German self-propelled anti-aircraft gun (SPAAG).

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Brazil, Germany, Jordan, Netherlands, Qatar, Romania, Ukraine.

Here is System information for the Flakpanzer Gepard German Self-Propelled Anti-Aircraft Gun of the Main Weapon System:
The Name is 2 × 35 mm Oerlikon GDF Autocannons.
The Caliber is 35 mm.
The Muzzle Velocity  is 1,440 m/s.
The Rate of Fire  is Each gun has a firing rate of 550 rounds/min. The combined rate of fire is 1,100 rounds/min.
The Maximum Effective Firing Range  is 5.5 km.

Here is Ammunition information for the Flakpanzer Gepard German Self-Propelled Anti-Aircraft Gun of the Main Weapon System:
The Caliber  is 35 mm.
The Cartridge  is 35×228mm .
The Basic Load  is 320 rounds anti-air ammunition and 20 rounds anti-tank.
