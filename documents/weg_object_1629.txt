This document describes the Fath-360 (BM-120) Iranian Close-Range Ballistic Missile .

The Fath-360 (BM-120) Iranian Close-Range Ballistic Missile is a shorter version of Fateh family of SRBMs. Its light weight and small dimensions allow several of these missiles to be put on a truck-based launcher.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Iran (Islamic Republic of).
