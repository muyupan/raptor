This document describes the SU-35S (Flanker-M) Russian Air Superiority Fighter.

The SU-35S (Flanker-M) Russian Air Superiority Fighter is the main production variant as delivered to Russia and China. Previously known as Su-35BM. Primarily air superiority fighter, but with secondary air-to-ground capability.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Russian Federation.
