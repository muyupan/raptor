This document describes the UBot-SCU B10 Chinese Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).

The UBot-SCU B10 Chinese Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) has been developed as a robotic scout inspection robot. The platform is ideally suited for under-vehicle inspection (UVI) duties. The robot can also be used to check large counters, underneath shelves and containers as well as the bottom of desks and other narrow and low spaces.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
