This document describes the 2S40 Floks Russian 6x6 120mm Self-Propelled Mortar System .

The 2S40 Floks Russian 6x6 120mm Self-Propelled Mortar System is a Russian-made 120mm gun installed on the armored Ural-4320 truck with a 6x6 wheel configuration. The main feature of the 2S40 Floks is its semi-automatic rifled gun that can fire all types of mortar shells and artillery rounds in the 120mm caliber.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
