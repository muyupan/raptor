This document describes the BA-93 Burmese 9mm Submachine Gun.

The BA-93 Burmese 9mm Submachine Gun is a Myanmar copy of the Israeli Uzi submachine gun. They feature wooden stocks, those which are similar to the original fixed stock from the Uzi itself. The firearm serves as a service gun, in using by the Myanmar police forces.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Myanmar.
