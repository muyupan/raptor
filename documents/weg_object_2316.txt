This document describes the SIG Sauer P220 German 9mm Semi-Automatic Pistol.

The SIG Sauer P220 German 9mm Semi-Automatic Pistol was Designed in 1975 by the SIG Arms AG division of Schweizerische Industrie Gesellschaft (now SIG Holding AG), and produced by J. P. Sauer & Sohn, in Eckernförde; it is currently manufactured by both SIG Sauer companies: SIG Sauer GMBH, of Eckernförde, Germany; and SIG Sauer, Inc., of New Hampshire, United States. It was offered for sale in .45 ACP, 9 mm, .38 Super (a rare model) and 7.65mm Parabellum (an even rarer model).

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Canada, Denmark, France, Germany, Iran (Islamic Republic of), Japan, Serbia, Sweden, Switzerland, United States of America.
