This document describes the Ithaca 37 American Pump-Action Shotgun .

The Ithaca 37 American Pump-Action Shotgun is also known as the Ithaca Model 37, is a pump-action shotgun made in large numbers for the civilian, law enforcement and military markets. Based on a 1915 patent by firearms designer John Browning for a shotgun initially marketed as the Remington Model 17, it utilizes a novel combination ejection/loading port on the bottom of the gun which leaves the sides closed to the elements.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Argentina, Taiwan, United States of America, Viet Nam.
