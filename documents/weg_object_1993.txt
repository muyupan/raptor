This document describes the Hopak-61 Ukrainian 7.62mm Assault Rifle.

The Hopak-61 Ukrainian 7.62mm Assault Rifle is a Ukrainian assault rifle developed in the mid 2010s from the AKM.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.

Here is System information for the Hopak-61 Ukrainian 7.62mm Assault Rifle:
The Alternate Designation is Hopak-61.
The Type is Assault Rifle.
The Caliber is 7.62mm.
The Manufacturer is Mayak Plant.
The Action is Gas operated.
The Muzzle Velocity is 715 m/s.
The Effective Firing Range is 350 m.
The Magazine is 10 or 30-rnd magazines.
The Sights is optical sight.

The Weight is 4.7 kg.
