This document describes the AMPV American Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The AMPV American Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) is the replacement for the M113 Family of Vehicles (FoV) within the Armored Brigade Combat Team (ABCT), comprising approximately 30% of its tracked vehicle fleet. The General Purpose variant accommodates two crew and six passengers, and it is reconfigurable to carry one litter and mounted crew-served weapon.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: United States of America.
