This document describes the HH-60G Pave Hawk American Combat Search and Rescue Helicopter.

The Sikorsky MH-60G/HH-60G Pave Hawk is a twin-turboshaft engine helicopter in service with the United States Air Force. It is a derivative of the UH-60 Black Hawk and incorporates the US Air Force PAVE electronic systems program. The HH-60/MH-60 is a member of the Sikorsky S-70 family.

The MH-60G Pave Hawk's primary mission is insertion and recovery of special operations personnel, while the HH-60G Pave Hawk's core mission is recovery of personnel under hostile conditions, including combat search and rescue. Both versions conduct day or night operations into hostile environments. Because of its versatility, the HH-60G may also perform peacetime operations such as civil search and rescue, emergency aeromedical evacuation (MEDEVAC), disaster relief, international aid and counter-drug activities.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: CFE Treaty, South Korea (Republic of Korea), United States of America.

Here is Variants information for the HH-60G Pave Hawk American Combat Search and Rescue Helicopter:
The HH-60A is Prototype for the HH-60D rescue helicopter. A modified UH-60A primarily designed for combat search and rescue. It is equipped with a rescue hoist with a 200 ft (60.96 m) cable that has a 600 lb (270 kg) lift capability, and a retractable in-flight refueling probe..
The HH-60D Night Hawk is Prototype of combat rescue variant for the US Air Force..
The HH-60E is Proposed search and rescue variant for the US Air Force..
The HH-60G Pave Hawk is Search and rescue helicopter for the US Air Force upgraded from UH-60A Credible Hawk..
The MH-60G Pave Hawk is Special Operations, search and rescue model for the US Air Force. Equipped with long-range fuel tanks, air-to-air refueling capability, FLIR, improved radar. Powered by T-700-GE-700/701 engines..
The Maplehawk is Proposed search and rescue version for the Canadian Forces to replace aging CH-113 Labradors. The CF opted for the CH-149 Cormorant instead..
The HH-60P Pave Hawk is Combat Search and Rescue variant of UH-60P, in service with Republic of Korea Air Force. Variant includes External Tank System and FLIR for night operations..
The HH-60W Jolly Green II is Combat rescue helicopter variant of the UH-60M for the U.S. Air Force to replace the HH-60G..
