This document describes the Sibirykov-Class (Project 865 Class) Russian Hydrographic Survey Vessel.

Sibirykov-Class (Project 865 Class) Russian Hydrographic Survey Vessel was ordered for the exploration of mineral resources at depths of up to 6,000 m. Each can carry up to two 18-ton submersibles. Equipped with 14 laboratories plus hydrographic, meteorological, magnetometric, geologic, gravimetric, and photographic facilities.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
