This document describes the MZKT-7930 Belarusian 8x8 Special Wheeled Chassis.

The MZKT-7930 special wheeled chassis was developed in Belarus the early 1990s. Sometimes this vehicle is nicknamed the Astrolog (Astrologer). It was designed to replace the ageing MAZ-543 special wheeled chassis.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Belarus, Russian Federation.
