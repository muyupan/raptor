This document describes the Fort-17 Ukrainian 9mm Pistol.

The Fort-17 Ukrainian 9mm Pistol is a semi-automatic pistol which was designed in the year 2004 by Ukrainian firearms designer RPC Fort.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation, Ukraine.

Here is Variants information for the Fort-17 Ukrainian 9mm Pistol:
The Fort 17 is designed for 9 x 18 mm PM cartridge..
The Fort 17 Curz is designed for 9 mm Kurz cartridge..
The Kobra is IPSC sport pistol, 9×18mm Makarov cartridge..
The Kordon is sporting pistol designed for .22 Long Rifle. 10 rounds box magazine..
The Fort 17R is Non-lethal gas pistol with the ability to fire ammunition with rubber bullets..
The Fort 17T is Non-lethal gas pistol with the ability to fire ammunition with rubber bullets..
