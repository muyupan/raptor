This document describes the MAN HX58 German 6x6 Heavy Utility Truck.

The MAN HX58 is a tactical military truck. Vehicle has a 6x6 configuration. This military truck is in service with Germany, New Zealand and UK.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Denmark, Germany, New Zealand, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, Viet Nam.
