This document describes the SAEGHEH Iranian Anti-Personnel Grenade .

The SAEGHEH Iranian Anti-Personnel Grenade is a high-explosive (HE), anti-personnel, projected grenade developed to extend the utility of the RPG-7 portable rocket launchers in service with the Iranian forces and is a direct copy of the Russian OG-7.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Iran (Islamic Republic of).
