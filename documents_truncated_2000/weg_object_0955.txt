This document describes the FTC-2000G Chinese Multi-Role Aircraft.

The FTC-2000 is a JJ-7/FT-7 with a new wing, a forward fuselage with side air intakes, and a glass cockpit. The engine, management, and mechanical controls of the JJ-7/FT-7 are retained.

The FTC-2000G is a multi-role combat fighter aircraft and one of the cheapest multi role fighters on the market with the aim to replace old legacy fighters like the J-7/F-7. It has 7 hard-points. It also features a diverterless supersonic inlet. It made its first flight in September 2018. Compared to the FTC-2000 trainer variant, the FTC-2000G is heavier, slower due to new wing design, and has less endurance than the FTC-2000.
