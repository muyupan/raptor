This document describes the Pantsir-S2 Russian 8x8 Self-Propelled Anti-Aircraft System.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Algeria, Equatorial Guinea, Ethiopia, Iran (Islamic Republic of), Jordan, Myanmar, Oman, Russian Federation, Serbia, Syria, Ukraine, United Arab Emirates, Viet Nam.
