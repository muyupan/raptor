This document describes the OFB Indian 4x4 Ordnance Factory Board Mine Protected Vehicle .

The OFB Indian 4x4 Ordnance Factory Board Mine Protected Vehicle is a Mine-Resistant Ambush Protected-type vehicle used by the Indian Army and the Central Reserve Police Force as an armored personnel carrier to transport personnel with protection from explosives and small arms fire.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: India, Myanmar, Nepal.
