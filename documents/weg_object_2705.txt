This document describes the DT-3P Russian All-Terrain Tracked Carrier .

The DT-3P Russian All-Terrain Tracked Carrier is a Russian articulated all-terrain tracked carrier. It is one of the smallest members of Vityaz family of articulated vehicles. It seems that is was developed as a private venture to meet a possible requirement of the Russian Army.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
