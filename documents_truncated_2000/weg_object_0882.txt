This document describes the AQV 100 Scalpel Ukrainian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The AQV 100 Scalpel Ukrainian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is a first person view munition carrying drone. Capable of carrying either a 1kg or 2.5kg payload over 10km. These systems originate on the battlefields of Ukraine and have proved to be highly effective against armour, concealed units and individual targets.

The flexibility this system offers is a long range precision strike capability at a unit level and payload flexibility to change rapidly for differing targets. These systems are also very difficult to jam due to their high speed and short warning time for engaged targets.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.
