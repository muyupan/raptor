This document describes the RGD-5 Russian Anti-Personnel Grenade .

The RGD-5 Russian Anti-Personnel Grenade is  Distance Hand Grenade or Time-delayed Hand Grenade), a post World War II, high-explosive (HE), anti-personnel (AP), fragmentation hand grenade, designed in the early 1950s, also made in Bulgaria, Albania and Georgia with a Chinese copy designated the Type 59.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Albania, Bulgaria, China, Georgia, Iran (Islamic Republic of), Russian Federation.
