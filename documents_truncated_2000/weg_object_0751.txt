This document describes the Aludra Mk1 Malaysian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The ALUDRA (Alliance Unmanned Developmental Research Aircraft) is intended for battlefield surveillance and reconnaissance missions.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Malaysia.

Here is Variants information for the Aludra Mk1 Malaysian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV):
The Aludra Mk1 is First production of the UAV. Three build and currently in service with Malaysian Armed Forces..
The Aludra Mk2 is Improved version of Aludra with better engine, payload and endurance..
The Aludra Camar is Aludra Camar made its maiden flight in July 2017 and its expected to be completed by 2019.The UAV has designed to carry out surveillance and mapping missions..
The Aludra SR-10 is Lightweight UAV designed for light surveillance role..
