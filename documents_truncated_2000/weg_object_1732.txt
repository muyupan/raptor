This document describes the KrAZ-6446 Ukrainian 6x6 Tractor Truck.

The KrAZ-6446 tractor truck was developed in Ukraine by AvtoKraz. It is a successor to the previous KrAZ-260V. The vehicle is designed to operate on all kinds of roads. The KraZ-6446 is intended to haul semi-trailers.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.
