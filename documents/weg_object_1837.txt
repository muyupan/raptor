This document describes the HJ-10 (Red Arrow 10) Chinese Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM).

HJ-10 (or "Red Arrow 10") is a Chinese ground-launched anti-tank missile developed by Norinco. It has a tandem high-explosive anti-tank (HEAT) warhead, that may penetrate 1,400 mm (55 in) of conventional steel armor protected by explosive reactive armor. The maximum range is 10 km (6.2 mi), with pre-or post-launch lock-on.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Malaysia.
