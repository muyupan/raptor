This document describes the PC-8 Ukrainian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The PC-8 Ukrainian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) was designed for security and industrial applications. This multirotor drone can stay in the air for up to 50 min with a 1.5 kg payload. Standard payloads include high-performance EO and IR Full HD gimbals. PC-8 offers maximum reliability and ease of operation with professional GCS and the highest quality standards.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.
