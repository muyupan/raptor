This document describes the Beechcraft 1900D American Regional Airliner.

The Beechcraft 1900D American Regional Airliner is a 19-passenger, pressurized twin-engine turboprop fixed-wing aircraft that was manufactured by Beechcraft. It was designed and is primarily used, as a regional airliner. It is also used as a freight aircraft and corporate transport, and by several governmental and military organizations.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Algeria, Canada, Colombia, Egypt, France, Myanmar, New Zealand, South Africa, South Sudan, Switzerland, United States of America.
