This document describes the ARK 40 Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The ARK 40 Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is a drone with an octocopter configuration. It has a redundant navigation system, flight control system, propulsion system, and battery system, and is equipped with an emergency landing system. With these features, the reliability and safety of Ark are at the top level in the industry. Moreover, the design of the propeller is optimized in cruise flight conditions to achieve a longer range. Ark is designed for short-to-middle range end delivery missions. Its payload volume is more than 50L, which makes it suitable for many cargo delivery scenarios.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
