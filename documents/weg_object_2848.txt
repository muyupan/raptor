This document describes the 1V22 / MP-22 Slovakian Amphibious Air Defense Control Vehicle.

The 1V22 / MP-22 Slovakian Air Defense Control Vehicles is an air defense command and control vehicle. Typically, this vehicle is used by the command staff of 9K331 "Tor" (SA-15 air defense units. It carries a telescopic mast that is mounted on the front left of the hull. The vehicle can be recognized by the presence of this mast, and four roof antennas angled towards the rear of the vehicle. Also, the 1V25 / MP-25 vehicle featuring radar data processor subsystems typically works alongside the 1V22.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: CFE Treaty, Poland, Russian Federation, Slovakia.
