This document describes the FV104 Samaritan British Armored Ambulance.

The FV104 Samaritan is the British Army armoured ambulance variant of the CVR(T) family. It has a capacity for up to 6 casualties.The Samaritan is one of the variants of the Combat Vehicle Reconnaissance (Tracked) family of armoured fighting vehicles developed by Alvis plc for the British military. The FV104  is equipped to fulfil the fire support role with a 76 mm gun, but also capable of fulfilling to a large degree the anti-tank and reconnaissance roles.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
