This document describes the MN-79 Vietnamese Anti-Personnel Mine.

The MN-79 Vietnamese Anti-Personnel Mine is a high-explosive (HE), cylindrical, plastic-bodied, anti-personnel (AP), landmine that is designed to wound or kill by its blast effect a copy of the American M14.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Cambodia, Laos (People's Democratic Republic), Viet Nam.
