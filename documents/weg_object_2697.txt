This document describes the CS/VP4 Chinese 6x6 All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV).

The CS/VP4 Chinese 6x6 All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV)  is believed to have been developed and manufactured by the Chongqing-based China Jialing Industrial Co Ltd (Group), a subsidiary of NORINCO. In addition to the 8×8 configuration covered in this entry, other configurations of the same/similar platform have been shown, including the 6×6 CS/VP16.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
