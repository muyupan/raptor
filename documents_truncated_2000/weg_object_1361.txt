This document describes the Kaliningradneft-Class (Project REF-675 Class) Russian Medium Seagoing Tanker.

The Kaliningradneft-Class (Project REF-675 Class) Russian Medium Seagoing Tankers were originally built for the Ministry of Fisheries. They were originally intended for support of the Soviet fishing fleet but were taken over by the navy shortly after completion. The ship is not capable of alongside refueling or solid stores underway replenishment. She has 10 tanks that can hold 5,263 tons of liquid cargo.
