This document describes the Challenger 650 Canadian Transport Aircraft.

The Challenger 650 is the latest member of Bombardier's Challenger 600 family of business jets designed for executive transport and special missions. Spacious cabins provide excellent passenger comfort as well as sufficient space for operator consoles and mission system electronics. Numerous cabin configurations can be implemented.

Our best-selling large aircraft platform combines world-class interior design with the ultimate value proposition. Widest-in-class cabin, worldwide reach, and the lowest direct operating costs make the Challenger 650 aircraft the winning choice.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Canada, China, Mexico, United Arab Emirates.
