This document describes the OGI-7MA Russian Anti-Personnel Grenade .

The OGI-7MA Russian Anti-Personnel Grenade is a shoulder-launched, high-explosive (HE) fragmentation, projected grenade an improved pre-fragmentation design of the 40mm OG-7V (ОГ-7B) projected grenade.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation, Ukraine.
