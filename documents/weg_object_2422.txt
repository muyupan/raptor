This document describes the Stugna-P (Skif) Ukrainian Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM).

The Stugna-P (Skif) Ukrainian Anti-Tank Guided Missile is an anti-tank missile system designed and manufactured in Ukraine. The anti-tank missile weapon system has been developed by the Kyiv-based Luch design bureau to compete with foreign models of the same class.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Algeria, Azerbaijan, Egypt, Georgia, Jordan, Morocco, Myanmar, Qatar, Saudi Arabia.
