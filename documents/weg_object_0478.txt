This document describes the ZALA 421-10 Russian Reconnaissance Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The ZALA 421-10 Russian Reconnaissance Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) combines all advantages of fixed-wing type UAV and also provides long endurance. The UAV also has the ability to land on water and waterproof construction provides advantages when using UAV at sea.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
