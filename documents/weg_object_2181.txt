This document describes the TMD-40 Russian Anti-Vehicle Mine.

The TMD-40 Russian Anti-Vehicle Mine is a high-explosive (HE), elongated, wooden, pressure-actuated, anti-vehicle (AV), blast landmine that is very similar to the TM-39 and TM-40 landmines produced in the 1940s.


This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
