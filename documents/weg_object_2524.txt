This document describes the Gear Up Russian 749cc Motorcycle with Sidecar.

Our bikes are happy cruising between 65 and 75 mph. Our 749cc engine combined with powerful low end torque is the perfect size to compliment the steering dynamics of our all steel sidecar. In addition to a 4 speed transmission, every Ural comes with a mechanical reverse gear.  Reverse can be engage by the foot pedal to the right of the rear brake. Every Ural comes standard with a lockable and water tight sidecar trunk.  It's the perfect size for extra gear, supplies, and whatever else your adventures may need.
