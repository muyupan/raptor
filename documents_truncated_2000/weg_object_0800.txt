This document describes the Shahpar II Pakistani Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Shahpar II Pakistani Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is a medium-range tactical UAV System with autonomous take-off and landing. It can carry various types of payloads integrated for reconnaissance and day & night surveillance. Other features include accurate lateral, and longitudinal trajectory control, mission planning, management & control, geo-referencing & geo pointing for terrestrial targets.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Pakistan.
