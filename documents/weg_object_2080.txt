This document describes the K6 South Korean 12.7mm Heavy Machine Gun.

The ST Motiv (Daewoo) "K6" Heavy Machine Gun (HMG) is the standard HMG of South Korean fighting forces. It is a locally-produced copy of the storied American M2 Browning Heavy Machine Gun and, therefore, utilizes the same form and function of its widely-used counterpart.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: South Korea (Republic of Korea).
