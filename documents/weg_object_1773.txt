This document describes the Ashok Leyland FAT Indian 6x6 Artillery Tractor .

The Ashok Leyland FAT Indian 6x6 Artillery Tractor was designed as a private venture and was aimed both at domestic and foreign markets. The vehicle was designed to be used as a gun tractor for up to 155 mm field guns and howitzers.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: India.
