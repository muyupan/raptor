This document describes the Lazar I Serbian 8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The Lazar I Serbian 8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) is named after the Serbian Prince Lazar, who headed Serbian Army in 14-th century. This machine has features of both mine resistant ambush protected vehicle and armored personnel carrier. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, Kenya, Serbia.

Here is System information for the Lazar I Serbian 8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) of the Main Weapon System:
The Note is The Lazar I Serbian 8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) can be fitted with various remotely controlled and turret mounted armament, including 7.62 mm, 12.7 mm machine guns, 30 mm automatic grenade launcher, 20 mm or 30 mm cannons. The first Lazar APC is armed with a 20 mm cannon and coaxial 7.62 mm machine gun..
