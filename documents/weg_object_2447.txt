This document describes the THeMIS Combat with GUARDIAN 2.0 Estonian Tracked Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).

The THeMIS Combat with GUARDIAN 2.0 Estonian Tracked Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) provides defense capabilities over short and medium ranges with high firing accuracy and it is a cost-effective solution for defense against asymmetric threats. The system is stabilized and can operate by day and by night. Operational functions include surveillance, target identification and tracking. Ballistic calculations for shooting are programmed into the main computer unit, allowing improved shooting accuracy.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Estonia, France, Germany, Netherlands, Norway, Singapore, Spain, Ukraine, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States of America.
