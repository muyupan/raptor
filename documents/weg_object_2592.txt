This document describes the BMP-K-64 Ukrainian 8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The BMP-K-64 Ukrainian 8x8 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) was developed as a private venture by Morozov Machine Building Design Bureau. Previously this vehicle was referred as BMPT-K-64. It is a conversion of the ageing T-64 main battle tank.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.
