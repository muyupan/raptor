This document describes the Krabi Class Thai Offshore Patrol Vessel.

The Krabi Class Thai Offshore Patrol Vessel is an offshore patrol vessel (OPV) of the Royal Thai Navy. She is a modified River-class patrol vessel and was built by Mahidol Adulyadej Naval Dockyard, with design and technology transfer support from BAE Systems Surface Ships.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Thailand.
