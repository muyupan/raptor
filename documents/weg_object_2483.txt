This document describes the M1064 American Self-Propelled Mortar.

The M1064 mortar carrier is an American tracked, self-propelled artillery vehicle, consisting of the M121 mortar - a version of the M120. Mounted on an M113A2 or M113A3 chassis.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: CFE Treaty, Chile, Egypt, Israel, Lithuania, Thailand, United States of America.
