This document describes the Grachonok-Class (Project 21980 Class) Russian Anti-Saboteur Vessel.

The Grachonok class, Russian designation Project 21980 Grachonok, is a class of anti-saboteur and large guard boats being built for the Russian Navy. Designed by JSC Vympel Design Bureau, the Project 21980 class counter-sabotage vessels are intended for the protection of naval bases, ports, and aquatic areas of the Russian Federation.

The boats are also used to fight against sabotage and terrorist activities in the sea, as well as render assistance to the Border Service of the Russian Federation in their efforts to safeguard the state borders.

The Russian Navy has commissioned a total of 12 Project 21980 class counter-sabotage vessels as of November.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
