This document describes the Sejong the Great Class (KDX-III Class) South Korean Destroyer.

The Sejong the Great-class, also known as KDX-III, are three guided missile destroyers of the Republic of Korea Navy (ROKN). The ROKN has deployed three ships with an option for three more which was taken up in December 2013.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: South Korea (Republic of Korea).
