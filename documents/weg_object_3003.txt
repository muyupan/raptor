This document describes the UH-1J Japanese Utility Helicopter.

The UH-1J is the improved licensed variant of the Bell UH-1H Utility military helicopter. Fuji Heavy Industries, Japan is licensed to produce the UH-1J. This variant includes a vibration reduction system, infrared exhaust suppressor, as well as other improvements. It is powered by a single Kawasaki T53-K-703 turboshaft engine, with two-bladed main and tail rotors. 

The UH-1H is an improved UH-1D with a Lycoming T53-L-13 engine of 1,400 shp (1,000 kW). 5435 built.. The Fuji-Allison T53-L-703 engine produces 1,800 shp.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Japan.
