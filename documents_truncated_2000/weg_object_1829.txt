This document describes the LTSD-4-3 Russian Laser Rangefinder/Designator.

The LTSD-4-3 Laser Rangefinder/Designator is designed for ground target reconnaissance, correction of artillery and air firing in daylight and at night including the use of high-accuracy ammunition fitted with laser homing heads when illuminating the stationary and moving targets, military equipment and offensive fortifications by a laser light from ground observation points. It may be also used in automated fire control systems.

SYSTEM COMPOSITION
*PP-20 transceiver
*Set for fastening PP on terrain
*1E77 synchronization unit
*1E67M accumulator battery (2 pcs.)
*Accessory set
*Transport case including two flexible packs for carrying
*Instruction manual

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
