This document describes the Type 01 LMAT Japanese Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM).

The Type 01 LMAT is a Japanese man-portable fire-and-forget anti-tank missile. Development began in 1993 at Kawasaki Heavy Industries and was accepted into service in 2001. During development, the missile was designated with the codename XATM-5. It was later known shortly as the ATM-5.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Japan.

Here is Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM) information for the Type 01 LMAT Japanese Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM) of the System:
The Name is Type 01 LMAT.
The Type is Anti-Tank Guided Missile.
The Caliber is 140.
The Manufacturer is Kawasaki Heavy Industries.
The Length is 860 mm.
The Missile Weight is 11.4kg.
The Total Weight is 17.5kg.
The Engine is Solid Fuel Rocket.
The Warhead is Tandem-charged HEAT warhead.
The Effective Firing Range is 4 km.
The Sights is Thermographic camera (Uncooled infrared detector).
The Guidance System is Infrared homing, CCD.
