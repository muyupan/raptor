This document describes the 3K60 Bal (SSC-6 Sennight) Russian Coastal Defense System .

The 3K60 Bal (SSC-6 Sennight) Russian Coastal Defense System  is a modern coastal defense system of Russian origin. It was developed in the 1990's as a possible replacement for the 4K51 Rubezh system and for export sales. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
