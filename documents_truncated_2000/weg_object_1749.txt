This document describes the MAN 35.460 German 8x4 Heavy Equipment Transporter (HET).

This heavy equipment transporter (HET) is equipped with a hydraulic loading platform. Probably the last in a batch of MAN 35.464 VFAK acquired from year 2002 onwards. On top a British JCB back hoe loader.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Eastern Europe, Northern Europe.
