This document describes the MDK-2 Russian Tracked Trench-Digging Vehicle.

The MDK-2 trench-digging machine (maschina dorozhnoy kopatelnoy) is based on the chassis of the AT-T heavy tracked artillery tractor, this first appearing in the 1950s. Production of the AT-T heavy tracked artillery tractor, details of which can be found in the All-Terrain Carriers And Prime Movers section, is complete.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: CFE Treaty, Russian Federation, Ukraine.

The MDK-2M is There is also an improved MDK-2M model, this having an increased working speed of 387 m³/h. Ditch depth remains 4.5 m, and width remains 4 m (top) and 3.5 m (bottom)..
