This document describes the Vaygach-Class (Project 19910 Class) Russian Hydrographic Survey Vessel.

Vaygach-Class (Project 19910 Class) Russian Hydrographic Survey Vessels are in service with the Russian navy. The vessels perform hydrographic tasks, installing navigation buoys, transporting cargo to remote coastal navigation stations and lighthouses, and other such tasks.
