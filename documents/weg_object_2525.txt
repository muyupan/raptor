This document describes the IMZ-8 Russian Motorcycle.

The Ural IMZ-8 latest model of Ural motorcycles with different modifications. The various modifications of this model are also known as “Tourist”, “Solo”, “Gear-Up”, “Voyage”, “Cobra”, “Retro” and “Wolf”. The Wolf was the first chopper motorcycle produced in Russia. Its production started in 1999, but due to low demand on it the Wolf went out of production in 2011.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
