This document describes the Type 79/85 (SVD) Chinese 7.62mm Sniper Rifle.

The Type 79/85 (SVD) Chinese 7.62mm Sniper Rifle is a chambered self‐loading sniper/marksman rifle, which is a direct copy of the Russian SVD.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Iran (Islamic Republic of).

The EM351 is The EM351 variant is the 7.62 × 54R chambered copy of the Type 79/85 offered by the Jianshe Industry (Group) Co Ltd for commercial purposes..
The EM352 is Variant of the Type 85 in its ‘Anti‐Riot & Counter Terrorismʼ catalogues..
The NDM‐86 is This model, which is indistinguishable from the military model, is offered for commercial sales..
The NSG-85 is The NSG‐85 is a product‐improved version of the 7.62 × 54R chambered Type 85..
The Nakhjir is Nakhjir (Iranian local copy)..
