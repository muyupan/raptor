This document describes the Fort -14TP Ukrainian 9mm Pistol.

The Fort -14TP Ukrainian 9mm Pistol  is accurate, reliable and simple to use. Special feature in design of this model is fixed quick-detachable barrel.
Due to its ergonomics, extended barrel and extended line of sight Fort-14ТP has great accuracy even with rapid firing, that is why Fort-14ТP is main pistol for officers of special enforcements.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.

The Type is Pistol.
The Caliber is 9 mm.
The Manufacturer is Fort.
