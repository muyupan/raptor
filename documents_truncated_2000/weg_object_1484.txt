This document describes the Sin Hung North Korean Patrol Boat.

These ships were under construction from the late 1950s until the 1970s. Very lightly constructed.
In addition, another 21 units of a modified version of the SIN HUNG were fitted with hydrofoils. The latter was built between 1981 and 1985.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea).
