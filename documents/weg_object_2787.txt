This document describes the PMC-90 Polish Armored Vehicle Launched Bridge.

The PMC-90 AVLB developed on the basis of PT-91 and T-72M1Z MBTs is able to carry out missions required for operations of combat forces, improving and increasing their tactical maneuverability. Main components from MBT have been adopted to provide interchangeability and the best logistic support. The comparable performance of the whole family vehicle system was the main task during the design of PMC-90 to make it capable to combat the characteristics of MBTs.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Malaysia.
