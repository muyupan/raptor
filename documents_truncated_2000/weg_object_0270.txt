This document describes the CJ-10A (DF-10A) Chinese Long-Range Cruise Missile.

The CJ-10A (DF-10A) Chinese Long-Range Cruise Missile is a second-generation Chinese ground-based land-attack missile. It is derived from the Kh-55 missile. It is reportedly manufactured by the China Aerospace Science and Industry Corporation Third Academy and the China Haiying Electro-Mechanical Technology Academy.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
