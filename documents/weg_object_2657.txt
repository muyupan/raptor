This document describes the Tiger 2065 Chinese Heavy 4x4 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The Tiger Type 2065 is the heaviest 4x4 armoured vehicle offered by the Chinese private company Shaanxi Baoji Special Vehicles. Available in both open-topped and closed configurations, the Tiger can be adapted for a wide range of roles, such as APC, patrol, police vehicle, ambulance, command post and weapons carrier. Since
its introduction at the 2012 Eurosatory exhibition, the Tiger has been sold to several militaries including those of the Bahamas, Bolivia, Somalia and Tajikistan.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Afghanistan, Bahamas, Bolivia (Plurinational State of), China, Laos (People's Democratic Republic), Somalia, Tajikistan.
