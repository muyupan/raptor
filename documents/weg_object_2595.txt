This document describes the BTR-3E1 Ukrainian 8x8 Amphibious Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The BTR-3E1 is an 8x8 wheeled combat vehicle designed to carry infantry troops, arms, and supplies on the battlefield while providing fire support from a turret-mounted gun. It can also carry out reconnaissance, combat support, and patrol missions. This APC is part of the BTR-3 family and is modeled after the Russian BTR-80 that is in widespread use, and it is intended to replicate the design features of the BTR-80 so the cost of the vehicle and training requirements are reduced. The BTR-3E1 was originally intended only for export, initially to Thailand.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Azerbaijan, Chad, Kazakhstan, Myanmar, Niger, Thailand, Ukraine, United Arab Emirates.
