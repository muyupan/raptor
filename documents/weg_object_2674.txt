This document describes the Anoa (APS-3) Indonesian 6x6 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The Indonesian Anoa armored personnel carrier was developed by Pindad. Its development commenced in 2004. This APC is named after an Indonesian type of buffalo. It is also referred to as the APS-3. The design of the Anoa has been copied from that of the French VAB, which is also in service with Indonesia.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Bangladesh, Brunei Darussalam, Indonesia, Malaysia, Oman.
