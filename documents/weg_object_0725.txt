This document describes the Vepr Ukrainian 5.45mm Assault Rifle.

The Vepr Ukrainian 5.45mm Assault Rifle was announced in 2003 as the first indigenous Ukrainian assault rifle, designed by the State Space Agency of Ukraine.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine.

Here is System information for the Vepr Ukrainian 5.45mm Assault Rifle:
The Alternate Designation is Vepr.
The Type is Assault Rifle.
The Caliber is 5.45 mm.
The Manufacturer is National Space Agency's
R&D Center for precision engineering.
The Action is Gas-operated, rotating bolt.
The Length is 702 mm.
The Barrel Length is 415 mm.
The Weight is 3.45 kg.
The Cyclic Rate of Fire is 600 - 650 rpm.
The Practical Rate of Fire is 40 - 100 rpm.
The Muzzle Velocity is 900 m/s.
The Effective Firing Range is 500 m.
The Feed System is 30-round detachable box magazine.
The Sights is Initial models of the Vepr assault rifle had adjustable open sights with a standard side mount for scopes and night vision sights. Later models have a Picatinny-type rail and are fitted with indigenous red dot sights..
