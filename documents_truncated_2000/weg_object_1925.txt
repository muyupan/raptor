This document describes the FHJ-02 Chinese 62mm Rocket Cluster Launcher.

The FHJ-02 Chinese 62mm Rocket Cluster Launcher is currently the lightest rocket launcher of the People's Liberation Army. The seven-tube launching tube is only 21 kilograms, and it is only 32 kilograms with seven 62mm rockets.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China, Ukraine.

The Alternate Designation is FHJ-02.
The Type is Rocket Cluster Launcher.
The Caliber is 62 mm.
The Manufacturer is NORNICO.
The Number of Launching Tubes is 7.
The Crew is 3.
The Rate of Fire is 7 rockets in 3.5 seconds.
The Maximum Range of Fire is 800 m.
The Emplacement Time is INA.

The Note is the FHJ02 is assigned to the chemical defense fire-breathing squad. Each squad is equipped with 3, and every 3 people operate a cluster rocket launcher. Among them, the first shooter and two deputy shooters can complete 7 rockets in 3.5 seconds..
