This document describes the Uran-9 Russian Tracked Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV).

The Uran-9 Russian Tracked Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) is designed to provide remote reconnaissance and fire support to a variety of tasks conducted by the counter-terrorism, reconnaissance, and military units in urban environments.

Fitted with a variety of weapons and sensors, the robot will improve the combat effectiveness of the infantry squads, while offering maximum protection to the personnel. The Uran-9 robot complex consists of four unmanned ground vehicles, one mobile command station, and a tractor for the transportation of robots.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
