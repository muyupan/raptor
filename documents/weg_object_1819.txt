This document describes the MV3 Chinese 6x6 General Utility Truck.

The MV3 Chinese 6x6 General Utility Truck was developed by First Automobile Works (FAW) to meet a Chinese army requirement for a general utility truck with a payload capacity of up to 10 tons. The MV3 will be the next generation universal transport/cargo truck, replacing Dongfeng EQ240/EQ2081 and Dongfeng EQ245/EQ2100.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.

Here is Variants information for the MV3 Chinese 6x6 General Utility Truck:
The CTM-131 is Chinese military designation. 6x6 configuration with double cab. 5 ton payload capacity..
The CTM-132 is Chinese military designation. 6x6 configuration with single cab. 5 ton payload capacity..
The CTM-133 is Chinese military designation. 4x4 configuration with double cab. 3.5 ton payload capacity..
The CTM-134 is Chinese military designation. 4x4 configuration with single cab. 3.5 ton payload capacity..
The PCL-161 is 122 mm truck-mounted howitzer (self-propelled howitzer, SPH) capable of rapid deployment, based on CTM-133 chassis..
The SR-7 is Modular multiple launch rocket system based on CTM-133 chassis..
The AFT-10 Carrier is Two HJ-10 missiles and electro-optical sensor fitted on CTM-133 chassis.
