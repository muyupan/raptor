This document describes the Panhard M3 French 4x4 Amphibious Armored Personnel Carrier (APC).

The Panhard M3 French 4x4 Armored Personnel Carrier (APC) is an armored personnel carrier of French origin. It was developed in the late 1960s by Panhard and complements the successful AML reconnaissance vehicle with an armored personnel carrier based on the same chassis. The M3 is a low-cost vehicle and rather basic for Western standards.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Algeria, Bahrain, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Burkina Faso, Burundi, Chad, Congo, Gabon, Iraq, Ivory Coast, Kenya, Lebanon, Mauritania, Morocco, Myanmar, Niger, Nigeria, Paraguay, Rwanda, Saudi Arabia, Senegal, Sudan, Togo, United Arab Emirates, Yemen.
