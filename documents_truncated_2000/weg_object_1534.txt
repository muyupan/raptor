This document describes the Shmel-Class (Project 1204 Class) Russian Armored Artillery Gunboat.

Shmel-Class (Project 1204 Class) Russian Armored Artillery Gunboat.  These are heavily armed river craft, similar in concept to the French and U.S. riverine monitors of the Indochina-Vietnam wars. The craft patrol the Russian rivers that border foreign states.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.
