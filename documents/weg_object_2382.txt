This document describes the M203 American 40mm Grenade Launcher.

The M203 American 40mm Grenade Launcher is a single-shot 40 mm under-barrel grenade launcher designed to attach to a rifle. It uses the same rounds as the older stand-alone M79 break-action grenade launcher, which utilizes the high-low propulsion system to keep recoil forces low.

The M203A1 is Canadian Version..
