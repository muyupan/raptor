This document describes the M-1985 North Korean 57mm Self-Propelled Anti-Aircraft Gun.

This twin 57mm automatic AA gun, which is evidently without a formal designation in the open literature, [though possibly designated M-1985] is a DPRK-produced system mounted on a modified YW 531 APC. It is generally similar to, but distinct from, the Russian ZSU-57-2 system.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea).
