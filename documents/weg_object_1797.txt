This document describes the MAN HX60 German 4x4 General Utility Truck.

The MAN HX is a range of tactical trucks specially developed to meet military needs. It replaced in production the FX and LX ranges. The MAN HX60 military truck has a 4x4 configuration.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Denmark, Germany, New Zealand, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
