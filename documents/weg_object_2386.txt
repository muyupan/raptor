This document describes the QLG-10 (Type 10) Chinese 35mm Under-Barrel Grenade Launcher.

The QLG-10 Chinese 35mm Under-Barrel Grenade Launcher was designed to be mounted on China's QBZ-95 assault rifle. It is modeled after Russian under-barrel grenade launchers such as the GP-25, GP-30 and GP-34.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
