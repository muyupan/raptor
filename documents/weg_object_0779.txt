This document describes the BLA-06 Russian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The BLA-06 (bespilotnyi letatelnyi apparat, or pilotless aerial vehicle) is an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) designed for military and civilian reconnaissance operations. The UAV is the first of Russian design with retractable landing gear, enabling it to operate from normal airfields or roads.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Russian Federation.

The Type is Reconnaissance Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).
The Manufacturer is NII Kulon, Moscow, Russia.
