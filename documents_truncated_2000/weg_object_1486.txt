This document describes the Skrunda Class Latvian Patrol Boat.

The Skrunda Class Latvian Patrol Boat is a class of SWATH patrol vessels used by the Latvian Navy. The main duties of the vessels include fisheries inspections, search and rescue, environmental protection, sovereignty enforcement, and participation in NATO and EU operations.
