This document describes the Yasin Palestinian Anti-Tank Rocket Launcher .

HAMAS Proliferation. The Yasin Palestinian Anti-Tank Rocket Launcher is a Palestinian anti-armor weapon manufactured by Hamas. It was named in honor of Sheik Ahmed Yasin, the leader of Hamas, who was killed by the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) on March 22nd 2004. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Non-State Actors, Palestine.
