This document describes the Albatross Taiwanese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The Albatross Taiwanese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) is also known as the Chung Xiang II, is a medium unmanned aerial vehicle made by National Chung-Shan Institute of Science and Technology. It is in service with the Republic of China Navy. The Chung Shyang II UAV can perform surveillance, reconnaissance, target acquisition, artillery spotting and battle damage assessment sorties for the military and perform other duties such as border patrol, and aerial reconnaissance for various government agencies. 

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Taiwan.
