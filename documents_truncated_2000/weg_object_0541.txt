This document describes the PCL-161 Chinese 122mm Self-Propelled Howitzer (SPH).

The PCL-161 Chinese 122mm Self-Propelled Howitzer (SPH) is a truck-mounted 122 mm self-propelled howitzer used by the Chinese People's Liberation Army Ground Force. The PCL-161 was designed to provide indirect fire support. It is much more efficient than towed howitzers and can briefly change its firing position.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
