This document describes the Sinpo Class North Korean Patrol Boat.

The SINPO class is a North Korean-built, steel-hulled version of the Russian P-6 class. Pendant numbers are in the 400 series. All were completed during the 1970s and 1980s.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea).
