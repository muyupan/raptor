This document describes the QLG-10A (Type 10A) Chinese 35mm Under-Barrel Grenade Launcher.

The  QLG-10A (Type 10A) Chinese 35mm Under-Barrel Grenade Launcher features a redesigned firing mechanism located at the rear of the barrel assembly that fits under the fore‐end with a loop‐type trigger, which sits directly under the host weapon trigger‐guard.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.

The QLG-10 is The QLG-10 Chinese 35mm Under-Barrel Grenade Launcher was designed to be mounted on China's QBZ-95 assault rifle. It is modeled after Russian under-barrel grenade launchers such as the GP-25, GP-30 and GP-34..
