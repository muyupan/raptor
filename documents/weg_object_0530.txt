This document describes the KH-179 South Korean 155mm Towed Howitzer.

The KH179 is a towed 155mm field artillery howitzer, transportable by a CH47C helicopter or a C-130 carrier. The KH179 provides a Longer range higher reliability and easier maintenance than the medium towed 155mm M114A2 Howitzer. KH179 uses the newly developed 155mm ammunition family. A new family of 155mm ammunition furnishes KH179 with further conspicuous increase KH179 with a further conspicuous increase in range and lethality in both direct and indirect firing.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Indonesia, Iran (Islamic Republic of), Myanmar, South Korea (Republic of Korea).

Here is Variants information for the KH-179 South Korean 155mm Towed Howitzer:
The FH-179 is The FH-179 was fitted with new sights. This howitzer used panoramic telescope for indirect aiming. There is also an alignment device that replaces conventional method of bore sighting. The telescope is also used for direct firing..

Here is System information for the KH-179 South Korean 155mm Towed Howitzer:
The Alternative Designation is KH-179.
The Type is Towed Howitzer.
The Caliber is 155 mm.
The Crew is 7 - 9 men.
The Elevation is 0 to + 69 degrees.
The Traverse is 49 degrees.
The Maximum Range of Fire (High Explosive) is 18 km.
The Maximum Range of Fire (Rocket-Assisted Explosive) is 30 km.
The Maximum Rate of Fire is 4 rpm for three minutes.
The Sustained Rate of Fire is 2 rpm.
The Double Baffle Muzzle Brake is Yes.
The Pneumatic Equilibrators is Yes,  two pneumatic equilibrators in order to reduce the recoil..
The Displacement Time is 5 ~ 6 minutes.
The Emplacement Time is 5 ~ 6 minutes.
