This document describes the Duke Class (Type 23 Class) British Frigate.

The Duke Class (Type 23 Class) British Frigate is a class of frigates built for the United Kingdom's Royal Navy. The ships are named after British Dukes, thus leading to the class being commonly known as the Duke class.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Chile, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
