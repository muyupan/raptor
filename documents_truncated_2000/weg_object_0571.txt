This document describes the PLZ-07B (Type 07B) Chinese 122mm Amphibious Self-Propelled Howitzer (SPH).

The PLZ-07B (Type 07B) Chinese 122mm Amphibious Self-Propelled Howitzer (SPH) Chinese 122mm Amphibious Self-Propelled Howitzer (SPH) combines the cannon and fire-control system from the Type 07B howitzer with the ZBD-2000 hull. The weapon enables longer-range fire support farther inshore with heavier ammunition, including laser-guided rounds.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
