This document describes the Armtrac 400 British Minefield Breaching Vehicle.

The Armtrac 400 British Minefield Breaching Vehicle is the largest and most powerful of the Armtrac range. With its hydraulically driven 3 meter Tiller rotor, it is capable of clearing 2,400 square meters per hour (depending on terrain & operator experience). With an effective tilling depth of 55cm the Armtrac 400 is designed to destroy & withstand 10kg Anti-Tank mines.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Ukraine, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
