This document describes the DZG-141 Chinese Rocket Launcher.

The DZG-141 Chinese Rocket Launcher is a further development of the PF11 weapon. Owing to its designation, it is believed that the DZG141's rocket is fitted with a HEAT-Multipurpose (HEAT-MP) warhead. Its export designation is BKM6.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
