This document describes the WZ-10 (Wing Loong-10) Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

The WZ-10 (Wing Loong-10) Chinese Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV)  is a series of unmanned aerial vehicles of the High-Altitude Long Endurance (HALE) type, featuring some stealth characteristics. The wing loong-10 drone platform features various configurations and is designed for both the Chinese military and export customers.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
