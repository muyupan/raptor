This document describes the Ural-43206 Russian 4x4 General Utility Truck.

The Ural-43206 is a 4x4 version, developed from the larger Ural-4320, which has a 6x6 configuration. It was developed both for the internal market, as well as the export customers. It is worth mentioning that the 4x4 version is less expensive than the larger 6x6. The Ural-43206 was adopted by the Russian Army in 1996. Its production commenced the same year. This high-mobility truck is used in large numbers as a troop or cargo transport.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: Angola, Colombia, Cuba, Georgia, Greece, Guatemala, Laos (People's Democratic Republic), Mexico, Philippines, Russian Federation, Uruguay, Venezuela (Bolivarian Republic of), Viet Nam.
