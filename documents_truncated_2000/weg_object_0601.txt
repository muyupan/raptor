This document describes the Type 70-I Chinese 122mm Self-Propelled Howitzer (SPH).

It is estimated that no more than 200 Type 70-Is were ever manufactured.  It was build to fill a gap in self propelled artillery in the Chinese army.  It is believed that the Soviet Union assisted in the development and manufacture of the Type 70-I because of the use of the Type 54 cannon which is similar to the M1938 / M-30.
The vehicle has no roof and thus no overhead protection for the crew of 6-7 soldiers.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
