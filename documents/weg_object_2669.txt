This document describes the ZZH-09 (Type 09) Chinese 8x8 Amphibious Armor Personnel Carrier (APC).

ZZH-09 (Type 09) Chinese 8x8 Amphibious Armor Personnel Carrier (APC) is equipped with the equipment required to conduct C2 at a brigade level. Like many of the support platforms based on this family, it is designed to have matching mobility and protection so that it can work with the formation on the battlefield. The platform is known to be in service and may have the designation ZZH-09, which stands for Armoured, Combat Service Support, and Command respectively.

This equipment has proliferated into the following countries: China.
